21. make active voice(buat kalimat aktif) - He is driving a car right now - they make appointment this afternoon - the doctor is examining the patient


Jawaban:

Those 3 sentences are already in active voice. Yet, if you want to change them into passive voice, then:

1. He is driving a car right now. -> Active voice

A car is being driven by him right now. ->

Passive voice

2. They make appointment this afternoon. ->

Active voice

Appointment is made by them this afternoon.

-> Passive voice

3. The doctor is examining the patient. -> Active

voice

The patient is being examined by the doctor. ->

Passive voice

Sure! Here's a conversation about a phone call for making an appointment with Mr. Gunawan:

You: Hello, this is [Your Name], Mr. Gunawan's assistant. How may I help you?

Caller: Hi, [Your Name]. This is [Caller's Name]. I'm at the restaurant for lunch and I would like to make an appointment with Mr. Gunawan.

You: Of course, [Caller's Name]. I would be happy to assist you. When would you like to schedule the appointment?

Caller: I should be back at the office around 2 o'clock. Would it be possible to schedule the appointment for 2:30 PM?
