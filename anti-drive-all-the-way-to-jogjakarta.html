Penjelasan:

9. zizah is going to ... a beautiful dinner tonight

= Zizah akan ... makan malam yg cantik malam ini

A. make = verb 1 ✅

B. making = verb+ing

C. maked = salah,

D. makes = verb+s

10. they are ... drive all the way to Alaska

= Mereka ... mengemudi sepanjang jalan alaska

A. going = verb+ing

B. going to = Future Tense ✅

C. went = Past Tense

d. gone = verb 3

​

Berdasarkan Rumus FUTURE TENSE (going to) :

(+) S + is/am/are + GOING TO + VERB 1 + O

1. zizah + is + going to + MAKE + a beautiful dinner tonight

= Zizah akan MEMBUAT makan malam yg cantik/indah malam ini

2. they + are + GOING TO + DRIVE + all the way to Alaska

= Mereka AKAN mengemudi sepanjang jalan alaska
