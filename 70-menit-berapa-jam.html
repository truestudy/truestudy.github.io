4. 3 jam 20 menit + 1 jam 70 menit = berapa jam​


Jawaban:

5 Jam 30 menit

Penjelasan:

3 jam 20 menit + 1 jam 70 menit

3 jam = 3 × 60 menit

= 180 menit

1 jam = 1 × 60 menit

= 60 menit

3 jam 20 menit + 1 jam 70 menit

= 180 + 20 + 60 + 70 menit

= 330 menit

330 menit = 330/60 jam

= 33/6 jam

= 11/2 jam

= 5 1/2 jam

1/2 jam = 1/2 × 60 menit

= 30 menit

Maka, 5 1/2 jam = 5 Jam 30 menit
