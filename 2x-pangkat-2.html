[tex]$\begin{align}2^{1+2x}~+~2^{3-2x}~&=~17 \\ 2.2^{2x}~+~2^3.2^{-2x}~&=~17 \\ \bold{misalkan~a~=~2^{2x}} \\ \\ 2a~+~\frac{8}{a}~&=~17 \\ \bold{kalikan~dengan~a}& \\ 2a^2+8~&=~17a \\ 2a^2-17a+8~&=~0 \\ (a-8)(2a-1)~&=~0\end{align}[/tex]

didapat akar-akar :


[tex]\displaystyle \bullet ~~~~a~=~8 \\ \\ ~~~~\bold{2^{2x}~=~8} \\ \\ \\ \bullet~~~~a~=~\frac{1}{2} \\ \\ ~~~~~~\bold{2^{2x}~=~\frac{1}{2}}[/tex]
