Jawaban:

Untuk mengetahui panjang BD, kita harus mengetahui panjang BC terlebih dahulu.

[tex] {bc}^{2} = {ab}^{2} + {ac}^{2} \\ {bc}^{2} = {12}^{2} + {9}^{2} = 144 + 81 \\ {bc}^{2} = 225 \\ bc = \sqrt{225} = 15[/tex]

BC = 15

Maka, panjang BD adalah:

[tex] {bd}^{2} = {dc}^{2} - {bc}^{2} \\ {bd}^{2} = {17}^{2} - {15}^{2} \\ {bd}^{2} = 289 - 225 = 64 \\ bd = \sqrt{64} = 8[/tex]

[tex] {AB}^{2} = {CB}^{2} - {AC}^{2} \\ {AB}^{2} = {41}^{2} - {9}^{2} \\ {AB}^{2} = 1.681 - 81\\ {AB}^{2} =1.600\\ {AB} = √1.600 \\{AB} =40 \: cm[/tex]
