25. what are the functions of the underlined words? dialog 3 : ...?bantu jawab please


DECLINING AN OFFER

The function of the underlined words is for declining an offer.

------------------

Explanation

'No, thanks' atau 'tidak, terima kasih' adalah salah satu ekspresi yang dapat digunakan untuk menolak dengan sopan suatu tawaran.

Ekspresi lain yang dapat digunakan untuk menolak suatu tawaran adalah:

That’s very kind of you but I can do it myself. Thanks anyway

I appreciate that but I can do it myself.

Thank you for your kindness but …..

Please don’t be bother.

Sedangkan untuk menerima tawaran, kita dapat menggunakan:

Yes, please!

That’s very kind of you.

I'd appreciate that.

Thank you.

Thanks a lot.

Underline are defined as lines made under writing both in letters, types of words, types of sentences, and so on, with the aim of stating that the underlined an important element. Usually, we can find the use of this punctuation in the exam questions that we fill. However, this punctuation function is starting to be rarely used, especially in typing text on a computer.
