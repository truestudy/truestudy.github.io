20. Tolong ya activity 9 dan 10


Understanding Text - Advertisement

Activity 9

1. What is the advertisement about?

The advertisement is about Open House of Glen Cairn Public School.

2. Why is the title called Innovation and Renewal?

Because the aim of the event is to share the company’s new vision.

3. What will happen if you arrive at 8:30 p.m.?

You will miss the event because it started at 6:30 p.m. to 8:30 p.m.

4. Where can you ask for further information?

You can ask through phone at 613-836-2342 or access their website on http://glencalmps.ca.

5. In your opinion, who are supposed to come to the event?

In my opinion, high school students should come to the event.

Activity 10

2. Free

Purpose: To tell people that the product is for free.

3. Guaranteed

Purpose: To tell that the product/service offer guarantee

4. Best-selling

Purpose: To tell that the product/service is a best-seller

5. Grab it fast

Purpose: To encourage people for buying the product as soon as possible

6. Anyone can do this!

Purpose: To tell people that the product is very easy, that everyone can do it

7. Cancel Anytime

Purpose: To tell that you may cancel the service/booking at any time

Pembahasan

Advertisement text adalah pengumuman publik yang biasanya dipamerkan di media cetak, tv atau internet untuk menawarkan produk seperti jasa/acara/produk.

Generic structure dari advertisement adalah:

Purpose - tujuan dari penulis iklan tersebut.Name of product - nama produk yang ditawarkan apa, produk/jasa/acara?User - produk ditujukan kepada siapa? siapa calon konsumen?

Pelajari lebih lanjut soal-soal yang berhubungan tentang advertisement text:
