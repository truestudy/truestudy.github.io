5. Prosedur meraut pensil menggunakan bahasa inggris dan terjemahnya


1. Insert a pencil to a available hole in pencil sharpener, press it a little. (Masukkan pensil ke lubang yang tersedia dalam peraut, tekan sedikit)
2. Then rotate it until the result is sharp as we want. (Kemudian putar hingga hasilnya tajam sesuai dengan yang kita inginkan)
3. Done. (Selesai)

14. Apakah termasuk Text procedure pada cara meraut pensil, dalam bahasa inggris?


Iya bisa, tapi sepertinya stepnya cuma sedikit.

1. First of all, You stick your pensil tip in the pencil sharpener.
2. Second, twist it clockwise so that the sharpener slices the pencil tip.
3. And then stop at the right moment, or the tip will tear apart.
4. And when you think it is done, release the pencil tip from the sharpener.
5. There you go! Your pencil has been sharpen!

How to Sharpen a Pencil

Tools:

PencilSharpener

Steps:

Put the pencil into the sharpener’s hole.Adjust with the width of your pencil, usually sharpener comes with two holes.When the pencil is inside, turn it several times to create a point.Sharpen the pencil over a trash bin.
