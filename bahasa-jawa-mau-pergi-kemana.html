Cut Nyak Dien was born in 1848 in the Kingdom of Aceh. She was born from a lineage that prioritized religion and a noble family line.

Her father's name is Teuku Nanta Setia, Her mother's name is Putri Uleebalang Lampagar. Her husband named Ibrahim Lamnaga has died and from her marriage with him they had a son, then she married Teuku Umar and had a daughter named Cut Gambang.

Cut Nyak Dien received good religious education and life skills from her parents and teachers. And she also got good knowledge from her Quran teacher.

On March 26, 1873, The Dutch first launched an attack on Aceh. The Dutch immediately burned the Baiturrahman Mosque. Cut Nyak Dien saw it and she did not want to remain silent.

She immediately aroused the sense of the Acehnese people's struggle. At that time, the Sultanate of Aceh was able to repulse the Dutch colonialists. But next, the Dutch launched another attack in 1874-1880. The Dutch colonialists succeeded in controlling the Aceh Sultanate Palace in 1874.

Because of that, It made the People of Aceh do not have a safe place to live. They Finally chose to evacuate the mothers and children, Cut Nyak Dien was part of the refugee group. Meanwhile, her husband and other men fought against the Dutch in order to seize the territory VI mukim of the Sultanate of Aceh.

In this battle, Ibrahim, Cut Nyak Dien's husband, died on June 29, 1975. This is the point where Cut Nyak Dien took a vow not to stop fighting for Aceh and destroying the Dutch.

He married for the second time with Teuku Umar, they married in 1880. This marriage was well received by the people of Aceh. From this marriage, they were blessed with a child named Cut Gambang.

The struggle continues with Gerilya tactics. The first step was taken by Teuku Umar by approaching the Dutch and building strong relations between the two, in order to gain the trust of the Dutch.

And everything reached its peak when Teuku Umar and his 250 troops "surrendered" to them (Dutch) and claimed to want to join the Dutch. This event took place on September 30, 1893.

The Dutch strongly agreed, and Teuku Umar was given the title Teuku Umar Johan Pahlawan. And gave full power to Teuku Umar to be the commander of the Dutch troops.

The strategy taken by Teuku Umar and Cut Nyak Dien was not without controversy. Many Acehnese thought they had betrayed Aceh. However, while continuing to study Dutch tactics, Teuku Umar planned to attack Aceh.

When the plan was approved by the Dutch, Teuku Umar and Cut Nyak Dien along with their troops and weapons complete with ammunition went to Aceh.

But apparently they never returned to the Dutch. This betrayal is known as Het Verraad van Teukoe Oemar or the betrayal of Teuku Umar.

The Dutch were very angry, and they launched a large-scale operation to capture Teuku Umar and Cut Nyak Dien. At that time, the Dutch was led by General Jakobus Ludovicius. However, Teuku Umar managed to destroy the Dutch troops. And General Jakobus was killed.

The Dutch are still looking for tactics to cripple Aceh's power. The Dutch paid people to spy on Teuku Umar. Then it is known that Teuku Umar will attack the Netherlands on February 11, 1899.

Because the plan was known, Teuku Umar died in the war. Even so, Cut Nyak Dien remained strong and continued to launch attacks on the Dutch.

Even though she isn't young anymore, she continued to launch attacks on the Dutch. However, the Dutch were supported with complete strength. And in the end Cut Nyak Dien was caught by the Dutch by storming her place in Beutong Le Sageu.

For her services against the Dutch, the Soekarno government awarded her a National Hero of Indonesia through Presidential Decree No. 106 of 1964 to be exact on May 2, 1964. This hero title was submitted by the Governor of Aceh Ali Hasan.

The Dutch sent Cut Nyak Dien and was exiled to Sumedang - West Java. Due to age, Cut Nyak Dien died in Sumedang on November 6, 1908. However, in 1959 her tomb just got discovered.

In the Custody, Cut Nyak Dien was nicknamed "Mother Perbu", because she was considered a woman who had a good religious understanding.

Cut Nyak Dien was born in 1848 in the Kingdom of Aceh. She was born from a lineage that prioritized religion and a noble family line.

Her father's name is Teuku Nanta Setia, her mother's name is Putri Uleebalang Lampagar. Her late husband's name was Ibrahim Lamnaga and from her marriage she had a son, then she married Teuku Umar and had a son named Cut Gambang.

Cut Nyak Dien received good religious education and life skills from her parents and teachers. And she was well-educated by her teacher who taught her to read the Holy Qur'an.

On March 26th, 1873, the Dutch first launched an attack in Aceh. The Dutch immediately burned the Baiturrahman Mosque. Cut Nyak Dien saw it and she did not remain silent.

She immediately aroused the sense of the Acehnese people's struggle. At that time, the Sultanate of Aceh was able to repulse the Dutch colonialists. But next, the Dutch launched another attack in 1874-1880. The Dutch colonialists succeeded in controlling the Aceh Sultanate Palace in 1874.

Because of the attack, it made the people of Aceh did not have a safe place to live. Finally they chose to evacuate their mothers and children, Cut Nyak Dien was part of the refugee group. Meanwhile, her husband and other men fought against the Dutch in order to seize the territory VI mukim of the Sultanate of Aceh.

In this battle, Ibrahim, Cut Nyak Dien's husband, died on June 29th, 1875. This is the point where Cut Nyak Dien took a vow not to stop fighting for Aceh and destroying the Dutch.

She got married for the second time with Teuku Umar, they got married in 1880. This marriage was well received by the people of Aceh. From this marriage, they were blessed with a child named Cut Gambang.

The struggle continued with Guerrilla tactics. The first step was taken by Teuku Umar by approaching the Dutch and building strong relations between the two, in order to gain the trust of the Dutch.

And everything reached its peak when Teuku Umar and his 250 troops "surrendered" to the Dutch and claimed to want to join the Dutch. This event took place on September 30th, 1893.

The Dutch strongly agreed, and Teuku Umar was given the title Teuku Umar Johan Pahlawan. And gave full power to Teuku Umar to be the commander of the Dutch troops.

The strategy that was taken by Teuku Umar and Cut Nyak Dien was not without controversy. Many Acehnese thought they had betrayed Aceh. However, while continuing to study Dutch tactics, Teuku Umar planned to attack Aceh.

When the plan was approved by the Dutch, Teuku Umar and Cut Nyak Dien along with troops and weapons, complete with ammunition, went to Aceh.

But they apparently never returned to the Netherlands. This betrayal is known as Het Verraad van Teukoe Oemar or the betrayal of Teuku Umar.

The Dutch were very angry, and they launched a large-scale operation to capture Teuku Umar and Cut Nyak Dien. Meanwhile the Netherlands was led by General Jakobus Ludovicius. However, Teuku Umar managed to destroy the Dutch troops. And General Jakobus was killed.

The Dutch were still looking for tactics to cripple Aceh's power. The Dutch paid people to spy on Teuku Umar. Then it is known that Teuku Umar will attack the Netherlands on February 11th, 1899.

Because the plan was known, Teuku Umar died in the war. Even so, Cut Nyak Dien remained strong and continued to launch attacks on the Dutch.

With her age that was no longer young, she continued to launch attacks on the Netherlands. However, the Dutch were supported with complete strength. And in the end, Cut Nyak Dien was caught by the Dutch by storming her place in Beutong Le Sageu.

For her services against the Dutch, President Soekarno, on behalf of the government, awarded her a National Hero of Indonesia through Presidential Decree No. 106 of 1964 to be exact on May 2nd, 1964. This hero title was submitted by the Governor of Aceh Ali Hasan.

The Dutch sent Cut Nyak Dien and she was exiled in Sumedang - West Java. Due to age, Cut Nyak Dien died in Sumedang on November 6, 1908. However, in 1959 her tomb was only discovered.

In detention, Cut Nyak Dien was nicknamed as the "Mother Perbu", because she was considered a woman who had a good religious understanding.
