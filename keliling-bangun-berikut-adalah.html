1. keliling bangunan bangunan berikut ini​


a. K = 5,5cm

b. K = 15,7cm

c. K = 33cm

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

a. r = 3,5cm, d = 2 × 3,5 = 7cm

K = ¼πd

= ¼ × 22/7 × 7cm

= ¼ × 22

= 5,5cm

b. d = 10cm

K = ½πd

= ½ × 3,14 × 10cm

= ½ × 31,4cm

= 15,7cm

c. r = 7cm, d = 2 × 7cm = 14cm

K = ¾πd

= ¾ × 22/7 × 14cm

= ¾ × 44cm

= 33cm
