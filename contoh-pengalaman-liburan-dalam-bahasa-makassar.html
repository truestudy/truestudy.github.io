4. Tolong berikan contoh recount text tentang berlibur ke Makassar minimal 4 paragraf


Last month my school friends and I went to Sulawesi from Jakarta for spending our school holidays. There were five persons of us. All of us were boys.

We left for Makassar on Monday afternoon. Firstly, we went to Soekarno Hatta airport, it was raining along the way and the road was a bit crowded; the car couldn’t move fast because the road was slippery. We were talking and singing while the driver was driving the car perfectly. In the Soekarno Hatta airport, we got the ticket to Makassar directly and waiting for minutes then flied to Makassar. We arrived in Sultan Hasanuddin airport at 8 p.m. then we looked for hotel, because we didn’t have any relatives, than we found a cheap and nice hotel near from the airport.

On the next day we visited all interesting places that we could visit. Firstly we went to ford Rotterdam, the Netherland ford. We saw La Galigo museum and we bought some miniatures that related to the “ford Rotterdam”.

Then after that, we took a taxi and we went to “ford Somba Opu”. It is around 7 kilometers from our previous place, ford Rotterdam. There, we found traditional house of some regencies in south Sulawesi. We took so many pictures in every traditional house, we saw traditional house from Tana Toraja, Soppeng, Bone, Wajo and any other traditional houses.

Then on the second day we spent our day to visit another interesting places, and they are as the city-tour of Makassar. for instances: Mandala Monument, Masjid Raya, Al Markaz Al Islami, Makam Diponegoro, and Balang Tonjong. We had other interesting places to visit, but we didn’t visit all the objects because we didn’t have much time. So, we went back to our hotel at about 04.30 pm.

On the next day, we visited Losari Beach. Unfortunately, it rained when we were going around the beach, so we decided to leave soon. Then at 9.00 o’clock, when the rain was stopped. We started to go to Somba Opu souvenir. Luckily, the weather was fine so could enjoy the journey and we could also spend our money to buy all souvenirs we wanted.

At the last day, we went to Makam Syech Yusuf in Gowa. We saw the grave and learned about its history. Then about 1 pm we left to the hotel and prepared ourselves for going back to Jakarta.

Then at about 16.30 we left the hotel and went to the Hasanuddin airport. We all very happy and excited. We hope to be able to visit Makassar and all those places again.

15. contoh cerita pengalaman liburan menggunakan bahasa sunda


Pada dasarnya ada dua macam mengenai cerita pengalaman, yaitu pengalaman pribadi dan catatan harian. Pengalaman pribadi adalah cerita yang dibuat dengan terencana dan menggunakan pola atau rumusan terentu. Sedangkan catatan harian adalah catatan yang dibuat secara mengalir saja tanpa direncanakan atau mengikuti pola atau rumusan tertentu, catatan harian dibuat berdasarkan kejadian yang terjadi setiap hari atau pada hari tertentu atau jika ada kejadian yang penting.

Jadi, dalam menulis cerita tentang pengalaman liburan, seperti telah disebutkan di atas, harus dibuat rencana penulisannya dan harus mengacu pada suatu pola atau rumusan tertentu yang sudah umum atau dikenal. Ada beberapa pola atau rumusan dalam membuat sebuah cerita yang dapat digunakan, termasuk juga dalam membuat cerita tentang pengalaman liburan, terlepas dari bahasa yang digunakan.

Contoh rumusan tersebut adalah seperti menggunakan atau berpedoman pada unsur intrinsik sebuah cerita atau menggunakan rumus yang sudah luas dikenal seperti misalnya rumus 5W+1H. Sebuah cerita dapat dibuat dengan berdasarkan pada unsur intrinsiknya yaitu unsur tema, latar, pesan, penokohan, sudut pandang dan alur cerita. Sedangkan rumus 5W+1H adalah membuat cerita dengan melibatkan unsur what, who, when, where, why dan how.

Di bawah ini adalah contoh cerita pengalaman liburan, yang merupakan cerita pengalaman pribadi, dengan menggunakan bahasa Sunda dan mengacu pada rumus 5W+1H :

Pangalaman Piknik Ka Batu Karas Pangandaran

Kagiatan anu pasti paling loba nu mikaresep nyaeta piknik, jalan-jalan atawa liburan, boh jeung babaturan boh jeung kaluarga urang sorangan.

Kitu oge kuring jeung kaluarga kuring, tara tinggalen unggal aya kasempatan keur liburan atawa jalan-jalan pasti ngalaksanakeun kagiatan anu paling dipikaresep ku sarerea eta.

Dina liburan semester ganjil kamari, kuring jeung kaluarga kuring ngayakeun piknik ka tempat anu sabenerna mah geus sering didatangan ku kuring jeung kaluarga, nyaeta ka Pangandaran.

Tapi dina ieu piknik mah, sanajan kuring geus sababaraha kali ka Pangandaran, kakara dina liburan ieu kuring ngajaran ka hiji tempat di Pangandaran nu ngaranna Batu Karas.

Naon istimewana Batu Karas teh ?. Batu Karas teh nyaeta hiji daerah di Pangandaran anu ternyata basisirna masih beresih keneh jeung masih jarang pengunjungna. Jadi kuring jeung kaluarga kuring bisa leuwih laluasa ulin di basisir Batu Karas anu masing kosong jeung beresih. Kukituna, sanajan geus sering ka Pangandaran, piknik ka Batu Karas teh keur kuring mah mangrupakeun pangalaman anu berkesan pisan.

Waktu harita, ti Pangandaran ka Batu Karas teh jalanna masih keneh can alus, jadi kuring jeung kaluarga datang ka Batu Karas ti Tasik teh henteu ngaliwat ka Pangandaran heula, tapi ti Tasik kuring ka Batu Karas ngaliwat make jalan ka arah kidul ti Tasik.

Kakara waktu rek balikna ti Batu Karas kuring ngaliwatan Pangandaran. Memang waktu harita mah jalan ti Batu Karas ka Pangandaran teh masih raruksak keneh, tapi kusabab kuring memang rencana rek piknik oge ka Pangandaran, sanajan jalanna ruksak oge nya dilakonan wae.

Demikian, contoh cara menulis cerita pengalaman liburan dengan menggunakan bahasa Sunda. Dengan mengacu pada salah satu rumusan yang digunakan, maka penulisan suatu cerita dapat dilakukan dengan mengalir begitu saja, mudah dilakukan karena mengacu pada rumus yang sudah baku dan sistematis, sehingga jelas apa yang harus ditulis dan kapan harus berhenti menulis.

Dengan mengacu pada rumus yang sudah baku dan sistematis, maka ada keyakinan pada penulis cerita bahwa cerita yang sudah ditulis paling tidak telah memenuhi syarat dari sistematika dan kelengkapan penulisannya. Jika dirasakan cerita tersebut kurang menarik, itu hanya masalah improvisasi atau seni pemilihan kata-katanya saja.

23. Contoh pengalaman Liburan Bersama Teman teman pake bahasa inggris


Holidays With Friends
On 16th of August 2014 my friends and I decided to take a vacation to the Kiluan bay, South Lampung. This vacation was planned to celebrate a farewell party for our friends who were going to continue their study in Java. Well maybe this was our last moment to have vacation together. Because we believed that it would be difficult to gather again.One day earlier, Friday August 15, we stayed at our friend’s home first. On the next day, we went there by motorcycle. The trip we travelled was quite difficult. So we spent 4 hours to arrive there. Having arrived there, we took a rest and prayed dhuhur. Afterwards, we crossed the Kiluan bay using a small boat to the Kiluan Island. It was an incredibly beautiful view. natural sea and clear sea water surface were the main attraction for me.

It only took 20 minutes to arrive the Kiluan Island. Once there, we rented a cottage and set up a tent for our night there. After Ashar prayer, we sat under the trees on the beach, enjoying our afternoon with the buzzing of the waves. We spent that night with joking and singing, of course, with baking fish for our dinner. It’s been 2 am we mingled with the beach. Finally, each one of us were crashed too, defeated by great drowsiness. Just the three of us who still survived until dawn came.

In the morning, a small boat picked up us to take us to see dolphins, Yup, There were a lot of dolphins, and this was the main attraction for tourists visiting here. However, I decided to go around the Kiluan Island alone. I was more interested in interacting with nature with my silence. It’s beautiful creation of my Lord, I thought. That day passed very quickly, we had to go home before night.

It’s a very short vacation, but I hope one day we can meet again and vacation together in a more beautiful place.

Kotoshi rebaran no yasumi ni tomodachi to sawaruna umi e ikimashita.

Mazu jyunbi shita atode issho ni ikimashita. Ni haku mikka no yotei detomari ni ikimashita. Watashi no ie kara sawaruna umi made otobaiku de ikimashita. Hachi jikan gurai kakarimashita. Totemo nagai jikan da to omimasu.cichurugu ni kyuu ni tomodachi no otobaiku wa kowaremashita. Watashitachi wa shurisuru tame ni shibaraku jikan yamemashita. Sono otobaiku wa ogoita ato de mata issho ni tsuzukemashita. Demo tochuu de nankai mo ugokimasen deshita. A~totemo taihen deshita. Tochu de nankai mo ugokimasen deshita. Nagai jikan ga kakarimashita kara,watashitachi wa sawaruna umi made 16 jikan kakarimashita. Tsuita toki totemo tsukaremashita kara sugu nete shimaimashita.

Tsugi no hi, asa ni natte tanoshimi ni shite ita taiyou ga noborimashita. Totemo kirei na taiyou deshita. Rebaran no yasumi ni sawaruna umi wa totemo nigiyaka na tokoro deshita. kodomo ya otona ya roujin nado takusan oyogi ni ikimashita. Sawaruna umi no shizen wa hontou ni subarashikatta desu.

Tsugi no asa, otobaiku de ie e kaerimashita. Totemo tsukareta ryokou deshita ga tanoshikatta desu. Mou ichido tomodachi to ryokou shitai desu.

I got 2 weeks to enjoy vacation during christmas and new year. I spent it visiting my family in the village. It was 60 kms away from the center of town.
I left for my village on Tuesday night. I rode my motorcycle. The street was really quite that night. So I could arrive soon in my family's house.

The first day I spent my day walking around my village. I met people who was my friend in the past. They were still friendly and welcome.

The second day I went fishing in the river near my house. I got 5 catfish. That made me happy enough because it was so long I had never gone fishing.

The third day I helped my father fixing the fence. The fence was broken. We had to renew it. I took bamboo and cut it.

The fourth day, I spent the day in the beach. The beach was 40 kms away. I went there with some old friends. In the beach I swam and went fishing. That was nice.
