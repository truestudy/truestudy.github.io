19. Respond to suggestions and offers given below 1. can I help you? 2. why don't you go and get something to eart? 3. why don't you joint us for lunch? 4. shall I bring a book to read? 5. why don't we meet at the bookstore tommorow at 5 pm? 6. let's eat together. 7. would you like a glass of water? 8. would you like me to do the ironing for you? 9. I will wash the car, if you like. 10. I think we should go and pick your father up from airport.


Suggestion expressions are expressions contain an idea or plan put forward for consideration or act of suggesting. We use offer expressions when we offer something or to do something for another person.

Pembahasan

1. Can I help you?

Accept : Yes, of course. Thanks.

Decline : No, thanks. I can do it myself.

2. Why don't you go and get something to eat?

Accept : Yes, I will. I will go to the canteen.

Decline : I can't go now. I'm still doing my tasks.

3. Why don't you join us for lunch?

Accept : Yes, sure. Thank for asking me.

Decline : I'm sorry. I will join you after finishing this.

4. Shall I bring a book to read?

Accept : Yes, you will. You can kill your time.

Decline : No, you will not. You prefer doing your homework to reading book.

5. Why don't we meet at the bookstore tomorrow at 5 pm?

Accept : Okay. I'll be there on time.

Decline : I'm sorry. I have an appointment with my mother.

6. Let's eat together.

Accept : Let's go.

Decline : Wait. I should wash my hands before eating.

7. Would you like a glass of water?

Accept : Yes. Thank you very much.

Decline : No, thanks. I drank a glass of iced tea before.

8. Would you like me to do the ironing for you?

Accept: Yes, of course. Please bring here your clothes.

Decline : I'm sorry. I can't. I should to go now.

9. I will wash the car, if you like.

Accept : What kind of you. Thank you.

Decline : Thank you, but. I washed it yesterday.

10. I think we should go and pick your father up from airport.

Accept : Let's go.

Decline : I'm sorry. I should go to the school. Would you mind to pick my father up? Thank you.
