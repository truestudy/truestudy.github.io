29. Answer the following questions briefly: who wrote the announcement? who is the announcement for? wht us the announcement about?


Answer the following question briefly adalah perintah yang digunakan untuk meminta penjawab menjawab dengan singkat. Arti dari answer the following questions briefly adalah jawablah pertanyaan-pertanyaan berikut dengan singkat. Jadi, jawaban yang diminta adalah jawaban pendek.

Pembahasan

Arti dari who wrote the announcement adalah siapa yang menulis pengumuman tersebut.

Arti dari who is the announcement for adalah untuk siapa pengumuman tersebut.

Arti dari what is the announcement about adalah mengenai apa pengumuman tersebut.

Contoh pada announcement berikut:

Several items have been found in and around the terminal door and toilet including keys, reading glasses, phones, watches, and even a few items of identity cards. If you have lost anything like that, please drop by the terminal help desk to identity your lost item. The items that are not claimed by September 22nd will be donated to a charity. Thank you.

1. Who wrote the announcement? The terminal help desk.

2. Who is the announcement for? Someone who has lost items in and around the terminal door and toilet.

3. What is the announcement about? Lost and found items.
