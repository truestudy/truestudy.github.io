13. bahasa Inggris, tolong bantu jawab bingung aku​


➊ The cat eats meat every day.

Meat is eaten by the cat everyday

➋ The farmer killed his good hen.

His good hen was killed by the farmer

➌ I haven't seen Mary for five days.

Mary hasn't been seen by me for five days

➍ The farmer will see his rice field tomorrow.

His rice field will be seen by the farmer tomorrow

➎ The woman takes her daughter to school every day.

Her daughter is taken by the woman to school every day

➏ I have bought a book of stories.

A book of stories has been bought by me

➐ Joe caught two birds last Sunday.

Two birds were caught by Joe last Sunday

➑ She wrote a letter last Sunday.

A letter was written by her last Sunday

➒ They have done their home-work already.

Their homework has been already done by them

PENJELASANSimpel Present TenseActive: S + V1 + OPassive: S + is/am/are + V3 + O + by + agent

Simpel Past TenseActive: S + V2 + OPassive: S + was/were + V3 + O + by + agent

Simpel Present Future TenseActive: S + will + V1 + OPassive: S + will + be + V3 + O + by + agent

Simpel Present Perfect TenseActive: S + has/have + V3 + OPassive: S + has/have + bee + V3 + O + by + agent
