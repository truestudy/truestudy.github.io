Deskripsi rusa dalam bahasa Inggris:

Deer are mammals, which means they give birth. Deer are part of the Cervidae family. The male deer, called stag or buck grow new antler every year. Most deer are born with white spots that will disappear within a year. Young deer usually call fawn. Deer have long legs, they can jump high and swim. Deer usually live in the forest. Deer are herbivores.

Deskripsi rusa dalam bahasa Indonesia:

Rusa adalah hewan mamalia, artinya mereka melahirkan dan menyusui anaknya. Rusa termasuk dalam famili Cervidae. Rusa jantan yang disebut stag atau buck , akan tumbuh tanduk baru setiap tahunnya. Rusa pada umumnya lahir dengan titik-titik putih yang akan hilang dalam waktu satu tahun. Anak rusa disebut juga fawn. Rusa memiliki kaki yang panjang, mereka bisa melompat tinggi dan berenang. Rusa biasanya tinggal didalam hutan dan mereka adalah binatang herbivora.

Untuk mendeskripsikan rusa, kita menggunakan teks dekriptif.

Apa yang dimaksud dengan teks desriptif? Teks deskriptif adalah tulisan atau kalimat yang digunakan untuk menggambarkan sesuatu. Tense yang digunakan dalam desriptif text adalah simple present tense.

Ciri-ciri dari descriptive text diantaranya:

Menggunakan simple present tenseMenggunakan action verb yaitu kata kerja yang menunjukkan suatu kegiatan yang bisa dilihat, contohnya walk, sleep, read, cook dan sebagainya.Verb yang digunakan yaitu attribute verb, seperti be (am, is, are)

19. Deskripsi 3 paragraf tentang rusa dalam bahasa inggris


Writing Descriptive Text - Deer

Deer are well known for being friendly and gentle animals. They are mammals, which means that deer are warm-blooded animals who has hair or fur and gives birth to live babies. Male deer called bucks, large males sometimes also called as stags. While female deer is called a doe and baby deer is called a fawn.

Deer live in many different continents in the world such as Asia, Europe, North America and South America. Their habitat is close or in the forest. In the hot weather, deer like to live in fields and forests which have tall trees for shade. Deer live in a small groups with other deer, this group is called herds.

Deer are herbivores animal. It means that they only eat plants and grass. Deer are not picky when it comes to eating. They can eat any kind of plants they find. Deer also enjoy berries and flowers in the summer. Meanwhile it the winter, they switch to eating bark and small branches since it is harder to find flowers.

Pembahasan

Deskripsi tentang binatang rusa di atas masuk dalam kategori descriptive text. Descriptive text adalah sebuah bacaan yang memberikan informasi mengenai suatu tempat, orang, binatang, dll. Pada descriptive text sifatnya lebih mengarah kepada penggambaran, pengungkapan secara detil dan penjelasan.

Generic structure dari descriptive text adalah:

Identification => Pendahuluan, yakni gambaran umum suatu topikDescription => Ciri-ciri tentang topik yang dibahas, sifat-sifat yang dimiliki, tampilan khas, dll.

20. classification rusa bahasa inggris


Deer (singular and plural) are the ruminantmammals forming the family Cervidae. The two main groups are the Cervinae, including the muntjac, the fallow deer and the chital, and the Capreolinae, including the elk, reindeer (caribou), the Western roe deer, and the moose. Female reindeer, and male deer of all species (except the Chinese water deer), grow and shed new antlers each year.in this they differ from permanently horned antelope, which are in the same order, Artiodactyla.
