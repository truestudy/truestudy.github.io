1. Why Didn't Madame Loisel Like With Party Invitation ?

Because she did not have any nice dress to wear for the party.

2. What Did Madame Loisel borrow from Madame Forestier ?

Madame Loisel borrowed some jewelry from Madame Forestier.

3. How Much Did Madame Loisel Buy The Necklace ?

Madame Loisel bought the necklace for thirty six thousand francs.

4. What Happened After Madame Loisel Returned the necklace?

Madame Loisel began the grindingly horrible life. Madame Loisel and her husband had to work hard night and day in order to be able to pay back for the diamond necklace.

5. Why did Madame Forestier look so sad after Madame Loisel told everything ?​​

Madame Forestier looked so sad because she had never thought that Madame Loisel began the grindingly horrible life over some imitation jewelry.

Pembahasan

1. Why do people write and send invitations?

To invite someone to attend the activity of event

Mengapa orang-orang menulis undangan?

Untuk mengundang seseorang menghadiri kegiatan atau acara

2. Why do you think invitations have become important in our society?

Because the invitation can maintain the relationship and convince the readers to attend the invitation.

Mengapa kamu pikir undangan menjadi penting di lingkungan kita?

Karena undangan dapat menjaga hubungan dan meyakinkan pembaca untuk menghadiri undangan

Generic structures of invitation card are:

Invitee: the person who invite the invitation Body of invitation: the content of the invitation

- Occasion: show the purpose of invitation

- Day or Date: show the date and the day of the event

- Time: show when the event starts

- Place: show the location of the event
