11. speech tentang effect of technology for student ​


Jawaban:

Penjelasan:

Technology has had a profound impact on education, and students today are growing up in a world that is vastly different from the one their parents and grandparents knew. Here are a few ways in which technology has affected students:

Increased access to information: Technology has made it easier for students to access a wide range of information and resources, both inside and outside the classroom. With the internet, students can find information on almost any topic they are interested in and can use online resources to supplement their learning.

Enhanced communication and collaboration: Technology has made it easier for students to communicate and collaborate with their classmates and teachers. Tools like email, video conferencing, and online discussion forums allow students to work together on projects and assignments, even if they are not physically in the same location.

Increased flexibility: Technology has made it possible for students to learn at their own pace and on their own schedule. With online courses, students can complete assignments and take exams from anywhere, at any time. This can be especially beneficial for students who have busy schedules or who need to balance their studies with other commitments.

Enhanced learning experiences: Technology has also made it possible for students to have more engaging and interactive learning experiences. With tools like virtual reality, simulations, and online games, students can experience concepts and ideas in a way that is more immersive and engaging than traditional methods.

Overall, technology has had a significant impact on education and has provided students with a range of new opportunities and resources for learning. While there are certainly challenges and drawbacks to using technology in education, it is clear that it has the potential to greatly enhance the learning experience for students.

29. 55% of student voted for maya as a class president. if 78 student voted for maya, how many student are there in the class? ​


Jawab:

There are 142 students in the class

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Given, 55% of students voted for Maya

Assume the total number of students be x

According to the question;

55% of the total students result in 78

Therefore, 55% of x = 78

 x = 78

x = 141.8 = 142 (rounded to nearest)

Therefore, the total number of students in the class is 142 students
