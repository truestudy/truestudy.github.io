17. On Sunday, the ratio of the number of customers at a furniture store in the morning to the number of customers in the afternoon to the number of customers in the evening was 3:5:9. There were 219 customers in the morning. What was the total number of customers at the furniture store on Sunday?​


Jawab:

1.241 customers

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

morning = 3 = 219 customer

afternoon = 5

evening = 9

the total number of customers at the furniture store on sunday?

morning = 219 ÷ 3 = 73

morning = 3×73 = 219customers

afternoon = 5×73 = 365 customers

evening = 9×73 = 657 customers

The total number of customers at the furniture store on sunday is 657+365+219 = 1241 customers
