29. 4000 m + 50 hm + 4 km =....km​


Jawaban:

4000 m + 50 hm + 4 km = ....km

= ( 4000 m : 1000) + ( 50 hm : 10) + ( 4 km )

= 4 km + 5 km + 4 km

= 13 km

JAWABAN:

4000 m + 50 hm + 4 km = 13 km
