What is the purpose of the flyer?5. How to get further information?6. Who are the targeted audience?7.How much is the ticket price? 8. Who is the organizer?


Below are the answer!

Both of the flyers are about a show. Flyer 3 is about a talent show, while flyer 4 is about a classic car show.Flyer 3 takes place on Thursday, 4 April at 7 p.m., while flyer 4 takes place on Saturday, 13 July at 9 a.m.The event on flyer 3 takes place at Trinity Homes' Cool Depot, while the event on flyer 4 takes place at Fitzgerald Park, Grand Ledge, MI.The flyers have purpose to inform people or audiences that there are going to be show events, so the readers can sign up/register or watch a talent show and an annual car show.The readers of flyer 3 can contact Marbi Dipastena via e-mail for further information. The readers flyer 4 can reach the Park Office by phone, e-mail, or visit their website.Flyer 3 targets audiences who have talents and like to present it. The flyer also targets people who love and enjoy to watch a talent show. While flyer 4 targets audiences who have classic car and like to show it off. The flyer also targets people who love classic cars.The people who want to watch a talent show must purchase a ticket for USD 5 (if they buy it in advance) and USD 10 (if they choose to buy it on the spot). While the people who want to enjoy a classic car show need to buy a ticket for USD 5.The talent show organized by Trinity Homes' Cool Depot, while the car show organized by Fitzgerald Park and sponsored by the friends of Eaton Country Parks.

Discussion

A Flyer is a piece of paper that contains information on something and usually distributed to people or displayed in public places. A flyer may or may not added by pictures and usually uses attractive colors to attract people. It is also a type of advertisement and those 2 flyers above advertise the readers about a show. The content of flyers should be brief but also comprehensive. As we can see in the pictures, the flyers have proper information that the readers need to know. The readers know about the name of the event, the location, the date and time, tickets prices, the details about the event, and an information of the organizer if the reader needed more information.

22. What is the difference between flyer, pamphlet, and newsletter​


flyer is a form of paper advertisement intended for wide distribution and typically posted or distributed in a public place, handed out to individuals or sent through the mail.

while pamphlet is a small booklet or leaflet containing information or arguments about a single subject.

while newsletter is a bulletin issued periodically to the members of a society, business, or organization.
