12. There are a few simple techniques you can use to consistently write great captions 1. Captions that tell and intrigue. 2. Finish the picture story with words.3. Strip the story. 4. Intrigue the reader. Pay attention to the following writing captions guidance! 1.Do not begin with the words a, an or the. 2. Use present tense to describe action in photo. 3. Give readers information they cannot get from just looking at a photo. 4. A caption should complete the photo. The reader should not have to look at the story, but should want to look at the story. 5 Write captions so they go from specific to general or general to specific. 6. Do not begin a caption with names. 7. When identifying members of a group, write "from left," not "from left to right 8. "Above" and "pictured here" are unnecessary. 9. Captions should not repeat information contained in the lead. 10. Name people only if they are important to the picture. Questions: 1. What does the text tell about? 2. What is a caption based on the text? 3. What simple techniques can you use to write great captions? 4. Mention what should you do to write a good caption! 5. Mention what shouldn't you do to write a good caption!​


Jawaban:

Questions:

1. What does the text tell about?

The text tells about a few simple techniques to consistently write great captions.

2. What is a caption based on the text?

Based on the text, caption is a text that intrigues the readers to read the story.

3. What simple techniques can you use to write great captions?

My technique is just to Keep It Short & Simple and I call it the "KISS" technique.

4. Mention what should you do to write a good caption!

1. Captions that tell and intrigue.

2. Finish the picture story with words.

3. Strip the story.

4. Intrigue the reader.

5. Mention what shouldn't you do to write a good caption!​

Do not begin with the words a, an or the.Do not begin a caption with names.The words "Above" and "pictured here" are unnecessary.Captions should not repeat information contained in the lead.Do not make the caption too long, Keep It Short & Simple (KISS).

Penjelasan:

Jawaban merupakan materi pembelajaran Reading Comprehension (pemahaman bacaan) dalam Bahasa Inggris.

1. Apa yang diceritakan teks tersebut?

Teks tersebut menceritakan tentang beberapa teknik sederhana untuk secara konsisten menulis teks yang bagus.

2. Apa yang dimaksud dengan caption berdasarkan teks tersebut?

Berdasarkan teks, caption adalah teks yang menggugah pembaca untuk membaca cerita.

3. Teknik sederhana apa yang dapat Anda gunakan untuk menulis teks yang bagus?

Teknik saya hanya Keep It Short & Simple dan saya menyebutnya teknik "KISS".

4. Sebutkan apa yang harus kamu lakukan untuk menulis caption yang baik!

1. Teks yang menceritakan dan intrik.

2. Selesaikan cerita bergambar dengan kata-kata.

3. Lepaskan cerita.

4. Intrik pembaca.

5. Sebutkan apa yang tidak boleh kamu lakukan untuk menulis caption yang bagus!​

Jangan mulai dengan kata-kata a, an, atau the. Jangan memulai keterangan dengan nama. Kata-kata "Di atas" dan "digambarkan di sini" tidak diperlukan. Caption tidak boleh mengulang informasi yang ada di lead. Jangan membuat caption terlalu panjang, Keep It Short & Simple (KISS).
