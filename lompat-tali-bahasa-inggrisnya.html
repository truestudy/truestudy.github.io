9. Cara memainkan permainan lompat tali dalam bahasa inggris


1. Choose the right rope. You should choose a rope with a length up to the shoulders when folded two. That long strap is enough to be able to jump comfortably without making our feet stumble.
The rope should have a hand-held grip, and not too heavy or too light.

2. Stand up and grab your rope. Grasp the rope handle in both hands. Spread both hands at least 30.5 cm away from the body and form a 45-degree angle to obtain a large, easy-to-jump rope arch.

3. Step by step. The rope should hang behind you so that the center of the rope touches the back of the foot.

4. Use both palm and wrist to swing the rope over the head. Do not move your arms, and try to restrict the movement of the wrist.

5. When the rope moves towards the front of your foot, jump. Stand with the tips of your toes, and jump with the base of your toes.
Try to keep movement in both of your ankles. Exercise becomes more difficult if your knees are bent.

6. Set the speed that works for you. For beginners, the rope should be swung so slowly that you have enough time to make the middle jump (a small jump between each leap rope Keep your back straight and keep the view straight ahead until you get the right rhythm.If you see your feet when you jump, chances are you will lose balance.
As your skills increase, the rope swings can be accelerated and the middle jump is eliminated.
As your skills increase, try to count the number of successful jumps done nonstop.

Answer:

Procedure text is a text which explains about how to operare / make/ do something.

The function of procedure text is to inform the reader about how to operate or make or do something.

Generic structure of procedure text:

- Aim / goal

- Tools and / or materials

- Steps

Here is the example of procedure text:

Aim: How to play jumping rope

Tools / Material: A jumping rope

Steps:

1. First, prepare your jumping rope. Make sure you choose the right jumping rope. The ideal jumping rope is a rope that reaches nearly your shoulder when it’s folded in half.

2. Then, stand up and pick up the rope. Hold the rope’s hand in each of your hands.

3. Place the rope behind you and turn the jump rope over your head.

4. When the rope is coming toward your front foot, hop over it.

5. After hoping it, the jumping rope will be behind you again. turn the jump rope over your head and hop it when it’s coming toward your front foot. Repeat this step over and over again.

6. If you feel tired, stop for a while and take a rest.

Rope jumping game is a game that resembles a rope arranged from a rubber band. This game can be used as a means of playing as well as sports.
