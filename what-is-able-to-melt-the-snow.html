Narrative Text is a text which tells something imaginative or something that only a bouquet of the maker and his only goal is to entertain the reader.

1. Type-the type of Narrative Text

There are several types of narrative text , as follows :

a. fables (Stories about animals are portrayed acting like humans)

b. fairy stories (Stories that are fantastic, full of wonders)

c. myth

d. romance

e. legends

f. personal experience (personal experience.)

2. Generic Structure Narrative Text

a. Orientation

Namely the introduction of a form : a story about the what (what is the story about), whoever the perpetrator in the story (who is the participant), where the story was going (where is the place setting) and when that story happened (when is the time setting).

b. Complication

That is the problem\/conflict in the story. This section is the core of the story narrative. Such conflicts can be distinguished :

- Physical Conflict

Namely the problems that arise because of the physical contact and everything related to physical.

- Natural Conflict

Namely the problems that arise because the perpetrator of the story dealing with the forces of nature.

- Social Conflict

Namely, a problem that arises because the actors that face each other. They met at the same time with different interests.

- Psychological Conflict

Namely the Problems that arise when the perpetrator of the story it's dealing with himself. The conflict between the good and the bad, between the greedy nature and tolerance.

c. Resolution

That is the solution or resolution of a problem or conflict that arises in the story. In the Narrative text, any problems that arise there should be the solution, can be closed with the end of fun (happy ending) or not bit of end up tragic, sad (sad ending).

d. Re-Orientation

This is the statement of the cover story and it's just optional. It consists of moral lesson, advice or teaching from the writer

3. Language Features

a. Using the sentence patterns the Simple Past Tense

b. Usually begins with adverbs of time (Adverbs of Time) . Such as : long time ago..., once, one, once upon a time.

c. Using the word liaison time

d. Sometimes there is a dialogue

27. What is the moral value in the story snow white


The moral value of Snow White :
This story teaches children many lessons. One of the most important lessons it teaches is the danger of vanity. The evil queen wants Snow White dead because she cannot accept the fact she is not the most beautiful woman in the kingdom. But it is this vanity that destroys her at the end.

The concept of real beauty is another theme discussed in this story. The evil queen may be beautiful on the outside, but she is wicked and ugly on the inside. Snow White, in contrast, is beautiful both inside and outside. Perhaps the mirror names her as the fairest of all not because of her physical beauty, but because of this real beauty. The queen will never be beautiful as Snow White because her heart is not pure. Therefore, the idea that real beauty comes from within is also discussed in this fairy tale.

This story also has another message for children. Just like in the tale of the Little Red Riding Hood, this story also contains the message don’t trust strangers.
