2. Victoria joined the Spice Girl before being a solo singer.

3. Victoria is a multi talented woman, she is an English singer, dancer, model, actress, and also business woman.

4. The text used past tense.

5. The generic structure of biography text:

Orientation (opening paragraph)Events (tells about events that happened to the person)Re-orientation (conclusion)

Victoria C. Beckham

Victoria Caroline Beckham is an English singer – songwriter, dancer, model, actress, fashion designer and business woman. She was born on April 17, 1974, in England. She became famous in the 1990's with the pop group "The Spice Girl"and was known as Posh Spice. In 1999, she married the Manchester United and England footballer, David Beckham. They have four children, three sons and a daughter.

After The Spice Girl split up, she pursued a solo singer career, but also started her own fashion range called dVb Style. Since this initial foray into fashion Victoria Beckham has brought out her own range of sunglasses and fragrance, entitled "Intimately Beckham"and a range of handbags and jewelry.

In addition she had written two best-selling books: one her autobiography and the other, a fashion guide.

These dried lavender sachets were then placed in closets, drawers, or near sleeping areas to release a pleasant and soothing aroma. Another method was extracting the essential oil from lavender flowers through distillation.
