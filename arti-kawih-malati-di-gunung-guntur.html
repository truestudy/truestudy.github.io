23. arti kawih peuyeum bandung dan kawih mojang priangan​


jawaban:

Kawih nyaeta salahsahiji rupa lalaguan Sunda. Aya sawatara rupa lalaguan Sunda, nyaeta saperti kawih, kakawihan jeung tembang. Ditilik tina rumpakana, kawih jeung kakawihan nyaeta karya sastra dina wangun puisi anu henteu kaiket ku aturan.

Sedengkeun tembang nyaeta karya sastra dina wangun puisi nu kaiket ku aturan, nyaeta aturan pupuh. Sanajan kawih teu kaiket ku aturan, aya sawatara unsur dina rumpaka kawih nu bisa digunakeun pikeun nilik eusi rumpaka kawih, nyaeta unsur tema, nada jeung suasana, rasa, sarta amanat.

Pikeun nilik makna tina hiji kawih, bisa ditilik tina amanat nu aya dina rumpakana. Jadi, makna tina kawih Mojang Priangan nyaeta nyaritakeun saurang mojang priangan anu leumpangna ngagandeuang siga nu taya karingrang teh nyaeta mojang anu denok, ayu, tur lenjang, sanajan make kabaya saderhana tapi nambah cahaya, jeung oge lucu sarta nyari, eta gambaran teh mangrupakeun ciri khas mojang anu aya di Priangan.

24. Cerita muncak je gunung guntur dalam bhasa inggris


Guntur Mountain is a stratovolcano type volcano located in Sirnajaya, Tarogong Kaler, Garut Regency, West Java, and has an altitude of 2,249 meters above sea level.


Thunder Mountain was the most active volcano on the island of Java in the 1800s. But since then its activity has declined again. Eruption is generally accompanied by molten lava, lapilli and other material objects. The recorded Mount Guntur eruptions were in 1847, 1843, 1841, 1840, 1836, 1834-35, 1833, 1832, 1832, 1829, 1828, 1827, 1825, 1818, 1816, 1815, 1809, 1807, 1803, 1800, 1780, 1777, 1690.


Guntur Mountain is adjacent to other mountains that surround the city of Garut. To the south of Mount Guntur, there is Mount Putri facing Mount Cikuray and Mount Papandayan, then to the west there is Mount Masigit, Mount Parupuyan and other mountains


Guntur Mountain itself has a Dipterocarp Hill forest area, Upper Dipterocarp forest, Montane forest, and Ericaceous Forest or mountain forest.


In the peak area of ​​Mount Guntur there is a very large and deep caldera that comes from the eruption. The characteristics of Guntur Mountain are generally sandy so that they are not overgrown with plants and appear barren. Some areas are overgrown with weeds and look like savannahs. At the top there are only a few cantilever plants that grow. Besides the cantilever, pine trees grow more on this mountain.


Yes, that's about a little introduction to Guntur Mountain. One of the famous mountains in the western part of Java, in the city of Garut.
