4. what is the purpose of the text analytical exposition? what is the purpose of the analytical exposition text?


║EXPOSITORY ║

Explanation

ANALYTICAL EXPOSITION TEXTGenre teks yang menjabarkan pandangan penulis terhadap suatu kasus tertentu yang dianggap penting untuk didiskusikan.

SOCIAL FUNCTION/ COMMUNICATIVE PURPOSETo convince the readers that there is an important thing to consider.

( untuk meyakinkan para pembaca bahwa ada hal penting yang seharusnya diperhatikan )

GENERIC STRUCTURE

1. Thesis

Bagian ini  dijabarkan sebagai awal pembukaan paragraf, menjabarkan rumusan pengenalan masalah yang didiskusikan berdasarkan cara pandang si penulis.

2. Arguments

Paragraf selanjutnya menjabarkan opini-opini si penulis  dengan berlandaskan informasi akurat berdasarkan bukti fakta guna mendukung argumentasinya.

3. Reiteration

Bagian akhir paragraf berisi kesimpulan / pengulasan kembali  tujuan dari pemikiran si penulis yang telah dipaparkan pada  bagian thesis.

LANGUAGE FEATURES { Unsus kebahasaan }Penggunaan conjunctive adverb ( konjungsi yang menjabarkan adanya hubungan sebab akibat,  perbandingan , ketidaksinambungan ( kontras )  

Contoh  : However, Additionally , So, meanwhile, consequently, as a result, and etc

Penggunaan simple present tense  

=> Karena berdasarkan fakta kebenaran, tenses keseluruhan menggunakan simple present tense.

Penggunaan passive voice ( tindakan aksi difokuskan utama )

Example : the children are exposed..........,  

Penggunaan  expression words

Contoh : in my conclusion.....,  i think that.....,

Answer

To convince the readers that having breakfast is very important for the human's body.

↪Untuk meyakinkan kepada pembaca bahwa betapa pentingnya sarapan pagi bagi kesehatan tubuh manusia.

EXPLANATION

1. Teks diatas merupakan analytical exposition text dalam penjabaran pandangan penulis terhadap pentingnya asupan sarapan pagi bagi kesehatan tubuh manusia.

2. Tujuan komunikatif dari teks diatas adalah untuk meyakinkan para pembaca bahwa betapa pentingnya sarapan pagi bagi kesehatan tubuh manusia.

3. Struktur teks diatas tersusun 3 bagian utama, yaitu thesis, arguments dan reiteration.

➡Bagian Thesis tertera rumusan / tujuan dari si penulis atas pentingnya sarapan pagi

➡Bagian Arguments tertera opini-opini si penulis dengan dilampirkan sumber akurat bahwa dengan sarapan pagi memiliki banyak manfaat.

➡Bagian Reiteration tertera penegasan ulang tujuan si penulis, betapa pentingnya sarapan.

PART : READING COMPREHENSION -ANALYZE THE GENERIC STRUCTURE IN THE RECOUNT TEXT

--------------------------

Jawaban + Penjelasan :

TRANSLATION:

Iwan pergi ke Jakarta dua minggu lalu. He mengunjungi tugu Monas. Disana, dia naik lift menuju puncak monas. Dia menikmati pemandangan kota Jakarat. Setelah dari tugu Monas, dia naik busway ke Taman Mini Indonesia Indah. Dia mengunjungi museum tehnologi sains. Dia juga menonton film di teater Keong Mas. Setelah itu , dia pergi ke Ancol. Dia naik kereta gantung disana. Dia kelelahan namun juga senang.

Pertanyaan : What is the purpose of the text?  ( Apa tujuan dari teks tersebut )

To retell iwan's experience in Jakarta.

Alasan : Teks diatas merupakan teks personal recount dimana menceritakan ulang pengalaman pribadi dari Iwan sewaktu di Jakarta. Dalam bahasa inggris, ketika terdapat pertanyaan "what is the purpose of the text?" , harap diperhatikan struktur bacaannya karena saling berkaitan selain informasi yang ingin disampaikan oleh si penulis. Masing-masing teks memiliki struktur dan tujuan (purpose) yang berbeda-beda.

Penjelasan:

RECOUNT TEXT

Genre teks ini menjelaskan kejadian penting, pegalaman yang terlibat di masa lalu, dan kisah bersejarah. Pada umumnya strukturnya terdapat runtutan kejadiannya.

SOCIAL FUNCTION/COMMUNICATIVE PURPOSE

~ To give the important information for readers

~ To retell the past events to readers.

KINDS OF RECOUNT TEXT :

1.Personal

menceritakan pengalaman pribadi

2. Factual

Menjabarkan kisah yang benar-benar nyata yang terkait sampai saat ini ,  terjadi berdasarkan aktual yang ada

3. Imaginative

menceritakan runtutan kisah fiksi yang ditujukan untuk membangun perspektif untuk si pembaca

GENERIC STRUCTURE  

1. Orientation

Paragraf pembuka untuk memperkenalkan plot cerita ( termasuk tokoh beserta orang-orang yang terlibat didalamnya, tempat dan waktu kejadiannya )

2. Events

Paragraf yang menjabarkan runtutan kejadian yang dijabarkan

3.Re -orientation

Paragraf yang menjabarkan kesimpulan akhir dari cerita.

LANGUAGE FEATURES ( UNSUR KEBAHASAAN )

1. Penggunaan  tenses lampau ( Past Tense )

2. Terdapat kata penghubung untuk merinci kejadian secara runtut, seperti : after that......, then......, and.........

3. Penggunaan  kata kerja aksi lampau, seperti : went, took, came...etc

30. 1. What is a recount text? 2. What are the structure of a historical recount? Explain each content of them!3. What is the purpose of a recount text?4. The generic structure of a report text are …. 5. What is the purpose of a report text? ​


Jawaban:

1. A recount text is a type of text that retells an event or experience that happened in the past. It usually tells a story in a chronological order, using simple past tense, and often includes personal feelings, impressions, or reactions of the writer.

2. The structure of a historical recount usually consists of three main parts:

a. Orientation: This part provides background information about the event, such as the time, place, and people involved.

b. Event(s): This part describes the main events that happened during the historical period being recounted. It should be presented in a chronological order and may include details such as what happened, when it happened, and who was involved.

c. Re-orientation: This part summarizes the events described in the previous part, and may also provide some concluding remarks or reflections.

3. The purpose of a recount text is to inform or entertain the reader by retelling an event or experience that happened in the past. It may also aim to convey the writer's personal perspective, feelings, or opinions about the event.

4. The generic structure of a report text usually consists of:

a. Title: The title of the report, which should be clear and concise.

b. Introduction: The introduction should provide background information about the topic, such as why it is important or interesting, and what the report will cover.

c. Body: The body of the report should present the main information, findings, or analysis of the topic. It may be organized into sections or subsections, and may include visual aids such as tables, graphs, or diagrams.

d. Conclusion: The conclusion should summarize the main points or findings of the report, and may also provide recommendations or suggestions for further action or research.

5. The purpose of a report text is to inform the reader about a topic or issue, and to present findings, analysis, or recommendations based on research or investigation. It may also aim to persuade or influence the reader's opinion or decision-making.

-Body: This is where the writer provides a chronological account of the events, experiences, or stories. It typically includes a series of paragraphs, each focusing on a specific event or experience.

-Conclusion: This is where the writer summarizes the events and provides a personal reflection or commentary on them.

4.The generic structure of a report text typically consists of:

-Introduction: This is where the writer introduces the topic and provides some background information about it.

-Body: This is where the writer provides factual information about the topic, such as its characteristics, features, or properties. It typically includes a series of paragraphs, each focusing on a specific aspect of the topic.

-Conclusion: This is where the writer summarizes the information and provides a final statement or recommendation.

5.The purpose of a report text is to inform the reader about a specific topic, often by presenting factual information in a clear and objective manner. It can also be used to analyze, describe, or explain a topic in detail.
