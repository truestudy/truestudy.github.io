Advertisement

Advertisement adalah salah satu short functional text yang mempunyai fungsi atau tujuan untuk mengiklankan suatu produk/ jasa/ atau mengenalkan sesuatu kepada masyarakat dengan maksud agar mereka membeli atau menggunakan produk yang diiklankan.

Generic Structure dari Advertisement adalah:

1. Title: berisi tentang topik utama atau judul dari iklan tersebut.

2. Explanation: Berisi penjelasan atau penjabaran dari produk atau jasa yang diiklankan.

Language features dari Advertisement adalah:

1. Menggunakan bahasa yang persuasif (mengajark)

2. Menonjolkan informasi dari produk.

3. Menggunakan bahasa yang menarik dan sopan.

5. in pairs, analyse the meanings of these correlative conjunctions in bahasa indonesia. ​


Correlative conjunctions always appear in pairs. They are used to link the equivalent sentence elements.  

1. Not only ... but also ...

Presents two related pieces of information: Menampilkan dua informasi yang berhubungan.

Sentence : She is not only beautiful but also smart.

2. Both ... and ...

Presents two related pieces of information: Menampilkan dua informasi yang berhubungan.

Sentence : Both My family and I will go vacation next week.

3. Either ... or ...

To indicate that there are only two options or that both of two options are acceptable or equivalent : Mengindikasikan bahwa hanya ada dua pilihan atau kedua pilihan tersebut diterima/sejajar.

Sentence: They like playing either badminton or football.

4. Neither ... nor ...

To talk about two or more things that are not true or that do not happen : Membicarakan tentang dua hal atau lebih yang tidak benar atau tidak terjadi.

Sentence: Her car is neither green nor brown.

Pembahasan

1. You use Either .. or .. to connect similar structures, such as two verb phrases, two adjectives, two noun objects, etc.

Examples:

I will see you either at home or in the coffee shop

You can either take a bus or drive  a motorcycle.

When a sentence has singular subjects and plural objects, verb follows the subject closest to the verb.

Examples:

Either my family and I am going vacation next week

Either my father or my mother is coming to visit me.

2. You use Neither.. nor .. to connect similar structures.

Examples:

Neither he nor she is coming to my party.

You are neither fat nor short.

When a sentence has singular subjects and plural objects, verb follows the subject closest to the verb.

Examples:

Neither my sister nor her friends are speaking Japanese.

3. You use Not only.. but also .. to connect similar structures.

Examples:

He is not only good looking but also friendly.

She not only cooked dishes but also cleaned the house.

When not only .. but also wants to connect two complete sentences,

Not only did she cook the dishes, but she also cleaned the house.

Not only is he good looking, but he is also friendly.

4. You use Both.. and .. to connect similar structures.

He play both soccer and tennis

He can both speak Japanese and Irish

When a sentence has singular subjects and plural objects, use plural verbs.

Examples:

Both my sister and my brother are very kind

Both her family and she are attending the seminar.

7. What job is required in the advertisement ?​


Jawaban:

• Account Service

• Creative Field

• Media Field

• Ad Production

• Marketing Research

• Marketing Consultant

Penjelasan:

Account Service / Account Management

Field of work that handles the affairs of marketing advertising services (from the advertising company). The main task of this field is to find new clients (companies / advertisers who want to use advertising services) by bidding or participating in tenders / pitching advertising work.

Creative Field

Field of work that handles the design / creative design, in the form of visuals (pictures / photos) as well as text (text) advertisements. This field is also responsible for providing guidance / guidance when the creative ideas it creates will be realized / realized into advertising material. This field is also referred to as "Bina Cipta" and its work is known as a copy writer and advertising visualizer visual advertisement.

Media Field

This line of work is responsible for determining the advertising media that will be selected / used to deliver advertising messages, schedule the appearance / loading of advertisements on the selected media, and calculate the costs required to use these media. This field is also responsible for managing advertisements on selected / determined advertising media. People who handle jobs in the media field are referred to as Media Planner Media Planners and Media Buyers.

Ad Production / Execution Field

Field of work whose job is to realize creative ideas in the form of advertising material. This field handles the making of images, photographs, recording / making radio advertisements, shooting / making film advertisements for TV or cinema and making Billboard advertisements. The production sector works on advertising materials based on ideas / designs created by the Creative Field.

Marketing Research

This field is responsible for finding information about market conditions, competition, consumer tastes, responses to advertisements that have been aired, etc., through research on the public / consumers. The findings of the study will be used as consideration in planning promotional / advertising programs.

Marketing Consultant

This field provides consulting / advisory services in the field of marketing, namely how to design products, determine prices, design packaging, choose distribution areas, how to deal with competition, etc.

Sumber: Duncan, Tom.(2005) Advertising & IMC, 2nd Ed., McGraw-Hill
