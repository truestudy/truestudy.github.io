8. contoh kalimat i can do them but i will not do them


Ten sentences 'I can do them but I will not do them':


1. I can cook, but I will not do it now in your kitchen.


2. I can do my homework myself, but I will not do it because I don't understand the lesson.


3. I can watch the television all night, but I will not do it tonight because I should wake up early tomorrow.


4. I can dance, but I will not do it in front of all classmates.


5. I can climb the trees, but I will not do it now with this shoes.


6. I can speak English fluently, but I will not do it in front of my teacher.


7. I can build a tent, but I will not do it without your help.


8. I can read these novels everyday, but I will not do it this week because I will have English tests.


9. I can run, but I will not do it now because you will be left behind me.


10. I can give my money, but I will not do it because I need the money now.


Dalam membuat kalimat, harus memperhatikan polanya:


subject + can + verb1 (base verb), but subject + will not (won't) + verb1.

Pembahasan :

Stating Capability

Stating Capability means stating the ability / ability to do something. In expressing ability / ability in English, you can use the capital "can, could, or be able" which means "can / can". Can which means can / can be used to express ability in the present tense.

Stating Willingness

Status Willingness which means expressing willingness, willingness to do something. To express willingness, you can use the modal –will/would which means "ingin /akan."

Will and would have something in common. The difference between the two modals is that the capital will is used for the present tense, would  the capital will is used for the past tense.
