Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Diketahui:

[tex]\sin 5x= \sin \frac{5}{6} \pi ,\: 0\leq x\leq 2\pi[/tex]

Ditanya:

Nilai x yang memenuhi persamaan trigonometri

Pembahasan:

Untuk persamaan trigonometri bentuk [tex]\sin x = \sin \alpha[/tex], maka penyelesaiannya adalah

[tex]x=\alpha +k.2\pi\\\text{atau}\\x=(\pi - \alpha )+k.2\pi[/tex]

Terlebih dahulu sederhanakan persamaan

[tex]5x=\frac{5}{6} \pi+k.2\pi \\x=\frac{\pi }{6}+k.\frac{2}{5}\pi \\\\5x=(\pi-\frac{5}{6}) \pi+k.2\pi \\5x=\frac{\pi }{6}+k.2\pi \\x=\frac{\pi }{30}+k.\frac{2}{5}\pi[/tex]

[tex]x=\frac{\pi }{6}+k.\frac{2}{5}\pi[/tex]

- k = 0

[tex]x=\frac{\pi }{6}+0.\frac{2}{5}\pi\\x=\frac{\pi}{6}[/tex]

- k = 1

[tex]x=\frac{\pi }{6}+1.\frac{2}{5}\pi\\x=\frac{\pi}{6} +\frac{2\pi}{5}\\x=\frac{5\pi+12\pi}{30} \\x=\frac{17\pi}{30}[/tex]

- k = 2

[tex]x=\frac{\pi }{6}+2.\frac{2}{5}\pi\\x=\frac{\pi}{6} +\frac{4\pi}{5}\\x=\frac{5\pi+24\pi}{30} \\x=\frac{29\pi}{30}[/tex]

- k = 3

[tex]x=\frac{\pi }{6}+3.\frac{2}{5}\pi\\x=\frac{\pi}{6} +\frac{6\pi}{5}\\x=\frac{5\pi+36\pi}{30} \\x=\frac{41\pi}{30}[/tex]

- k = 4

- k = 5
