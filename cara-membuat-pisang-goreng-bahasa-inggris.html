2. Cara membuat pisang goreng dalam bahasa inggris


Jawaban:

How to Make Fried Bananas

Ingredients:

- 4 ripe bananas

- 1/2 cup all-purpose flour

- 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

- 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg

- 1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom

- 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger

- 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves

- 1/2 teaspoon salt

- 1/2 cup white sugar

- 1/2 cup vegetable oil

Instructions:

1. Peel the bananas and cut them into 1/2-inch thick slices.

2. In a shallow bowl, mix together the flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, cardamom, ginger, cloves, and salt.

3. In a separate shallow bowl, mix together the sugar and oil.

4. Dip the banana slices into the flour mixture, then into the sugar and oil mixture.

5. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the banana slices and fry until golden brown, about 2 minutes per side.

6. Remove the fried bananas from the skillet and place on a plate lined with paper towels to absorb any excess oil.

7. Serve warm and enjoy!

13. Cara membuat pisang goreng dalam bahasa inggris dan terjemahanya​


Jawaban:

FRIED BANANA

Inggredients: (bahan-bahan)

3-5 banana (3-5 buah pisang)

Flour and rice flour (ratio 3:2) (tepung terigu dan tepung beras dengan perbandingan 3:2 untuk tepung terigu)

A pinch of salt (sejumput garam)

2 table spoon of sugar (2 sendok makan gula)

1 egg (1 butir telur)

cold water (air es)

oil (minyak)

How to make it: (cara membuat:)

1. First, mix all the inggredients (flour, rice flour, salt, sugar, egg, and cold water) in the bowl. (Pertama, campurkan semua bahan (tepung terigu, tepung beras, garam, gula, telur, dan air es) ke dalam mangkok.)

2. Cut the bananas into pieces. (Potong-potong pisang.)

3. Then, put them into the dough. (Lalu, masukkan ke dalam adonan.)

4. Fry them with hot oil until golden brown. (Goreng dengan minyak panas sampai coklat keemasan.)

Penjelasan:

Procedure text adalah langkah-langkah dalam membuat sesuatu hal (bisa masakan atau kerajinan tangan).

Formula procedure text:

The title (judul)Inggredients/tools (bahan-bahan atau alat-alat)Steps (langkah-langkah)Dapat menyertakan kata penghubung numerik seperti: first, then, after that, the last, dan lain-lain.

How to Make Banana Chese

Ingredients/materials :
6 medium-sized bananas, peeled
3 tbsp rice flour
3 tbsp wheat flour
1/2 tsp vanilla essence
2 tbsp milk creamer
grated cheese
Oil for deep-frying
A pinch of salt
A pinch of sugar

Steps :
1.First, mix sugar, rice flour and wheat flour with water.
2.Then soak bananas in the batter. Heat the oil in a large wok.
3.Then drop one banana at a time into the oil. Cook only two bananas at a time as the temperature of the oil will be drop if there are too many bananas.
4.When bananas are crisp and golden brown, remove from oil and drain on a paper towel and put aside.
5.Pour the milk creamer on top of the fried bananas.
6.Finally, coat it with cheese.
