Soal nomor 1 merupakan contoh dan sudah ada jawabannya.
Lina: “Look! The trash is everywhere. The classroom is very dirty.”
Dayu: “Pick up the trash and put them in the bin.” (what to do)
Udin: “Don’t burn the trash, it will cause air pollution.” (what not to do)

Situation 2:
Edo, Dayu, and Lina are on the way to school and they are going to have a flag ceremony.
Siti: “It’s nearly 7 o’clock now. Remember, we have a flag ceremony today.”
Udin: “We should walk faster.“(what to do)
Lina: “Don't walk while playing your gadget. It will make us walk slowly.”(what not to do).

Situation 3:
Dayu, Siti, Edo are sitting in the canteen during the break to have their lunch.
Dayu: “I’m very hungry, let’s get something for lunch.”
Siti: “You should wash your hands before eating your lunch.” (what to do)
Edo: “Don't eat too much spicy food. Or you will get stomachache.” (what not to do)

Situation 4:
The class is over.
Udin: “Well friends, it’s time to go home.”
Beni: “You should go home straightly.” (what to do)
Lina: “Don’t drop by after school. Your parents will be worried.” (what not to do)

Situation 5:
Siti, Udin, and Lina are shopping in the traditional market.
Siti: “I need some chicken.”
Udin: “You should buy it at the trusted butcher.” (what to do)
Lina: “Don’t just buy it because it is cheap.” (what not to do)

Pembahasan

Pada exercise ini, siswa diminta untuk menuliskan dan menjabarkan rutinitas yang sudah dilakukan oleh Dayu, Lina, Siti, dan kawan-kawan berdasarkan perspektif seseorang yang sudah disebutkan.

Daily routines adalah kegiatan yang dilakukan sehari-hari atau biasa disebut rutinitas keseharian seseorang. Dalam menuliskan daily routines, kita harus menggunakan simple present tense.

Simple Present tense memiliki formula sebagai berikut:

S + V1 atau S + V1+s/es

NB:

V1 untuk subject I, You, They, dan We

V1+s/es untuk subject He, She, dan It

Here are what Dayu, Lina, and Siti do as their routines according to Udin.
1. They are never late to class.
2. They walk to school, but they always get to school on time.
3. They rarely take public transport.
4. They often speak English to each other, to their friends, and to the English teachers.
5. They ask questions in English.

Kutipan Udin:
