2. jika keliling layang layang 54cm maka luas layang-layang tersebut....​


Menentukan panjang AB

2(AB + CD) = K

2(AB + 17) = 54

AB + 17 = 54/2

AB + 17 = 27

AB = 27 - 17

AB = 10 cm

[tex] \: \: [/tex]

Menentukan panjang AO

AO² = AB² - BO²

AO² = 10² - 6²

AO² = 100 - 36

AO² = 64

AO = √64

AO = 8 cm

[tex] \: [/tex]

Menentukan panjang CO

CO² = CD² - BO²

CO² = 17² - 6²

CO² = 289 - 36

CO² = 253

CO = √253 cm

[tex] \: [/tex]

L = ½ × BC × AC

L = 6 × (8 + √253)
