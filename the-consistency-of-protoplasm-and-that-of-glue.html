Teks pada soal di atas termasuk jenis procedure text. Tujuan dari procedure text adalah untuk memberitahu pembaca bagaimana melakukan atau membuat sesuatu melalui urutan tindakan atau langkah-langkah.  

Generic Structure of Procedure Text  

Goal: memberikan informasi tentang maksud dan tujuan prosedur dan memprediksi suatu kesimpulan. (provides information about the purpose and objectives of the procedure and predicts a conclusion)  Materials: berisi daftar materi atau bahan-bahan yang dibutuhkan untuk melakukan suatu prosedur atau langkah-langkah. (contains a list of materials or materials needed to carry out a procedure or steps)  Steps: daftar urutan instruksi/aktivitas untuk mencapai tujuan dalam urutan langkah yang benar. (list the sequence of instructions / activities to reach the destination in the correct sequence of steps)  

Language features of Procedure Text  

Mengunakan Simple Present Tense (S+V1)  Berbentuk imperative/bentuk perintah,  Menggunakan action verbs, contoh: make, take, boil, cook,  Menggunakan temporal conjunctions, contoh: First, then, next, after that, last

28. Tolong carii kan yang V1 dongg... How To Make A Frame Of Photo From Cardboard The cardboard that we often consider garbage can be used as craft material, one of which is a photo frame. The materials and tools used are very simple and easy to obtain. For tools and materials prepare cardboard, pencil or markers, scissors or cutter, glue, ruler, string or thread, clear plastic and prepare accessories such as flannel or gift paper as decoration. Then the method is first, prepare the cardboard to be made into a frame, then cut to the desired size depending on the size of the photo that will be placed in the frame. After that, specify the width of the frame to be made. The next step is to punch holes in the center of the cardboard that will be used as a place to display photos. The method is quite easy, paste it the photo right in the middle of the cardboard. After that, make a line following the size of the photo, then cut the cardboard following the lines that have been made. Then, draw a line inside the frame (inner liner). The goal is that the results of the frame look better. Next, decorate the photo frame. You are free to express ideas so that the picture frame looks beautiful. The trick is easy by attaching gift paper /flannel cloth to the frame that has been given glue. Then make the back of the frame, by cutting cardboard suitable the size of a frame that has been made. Then apply glue on each corner of the frame and glue all parts, the outer frame, the inner liner, and the photo. so that the frame is durable, you can add a protective plastic clear top of the photo. And finally, punch the right and left sides of the top of the frame, use a rope or thread to hang the finished frame on the wall. The following is how to make a cardboard frame that is simple handicraft with easy material and you can create a variety of frame shapes according to the creativity you have.
