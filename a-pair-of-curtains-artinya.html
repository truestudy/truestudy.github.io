7. 21. The lady is wearing a pair of sporty shoes. What is the negative form of the sentence ? A. The lady was wearing a pair of sporty shoes. B. The lady will wear a pair of sporty shoes c. The lady isn't wearing a pair of sporty shoes d. Is The lady wearing a pair of sporty shoes ?


Jawaban:

Penjelasan:

soal:

21. The lady is wearing a pair of sporty shoes. What is the negative form of the sentence ?

artinya:

21. Wanita itu memakai sepasang sepatu olahraga. Bentuk kalimat negatif dari kalimat tersebut adalah?

penjelasan:

Bentuk kalimat negatif ditandai dengan adanya kata tidak/bukan (NOT), yang menunjukan bahwa kalimat tersebut berlawanan dari kalimat positif.

Sehingga kalimat negatif dari soal adalah: wanita itu tidak memakai sepasang sepatu olahraga, kemudian diterjemahkan ke dalam bahasa inggris menjadi: the lady is not wearing a pair of sporty shoes, kemudian kalimat ini dapat disingkat menjadi the lady isn't wearing a pair of sporty shoes.

Jadi jawaban yang tepat adalah c. The lady isn't wearing a pair of sporty shoes.

16. A pair of shoes cost $82 more than a pair of cufflinks. If 3 pairs of shoes and 3 pairs of cufflinks cost $2904, find the cost of 1 pair of cufflinks.​


Jawab:
A pair of cufflinks costs $443

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Given that
shoes = s, cufflinks = c
shoes = cufflinks + 82
s = c + 82
→ s - c = 82
3 shoes+3 cufflinks = $2904
3s + 3c = 2904
factorise
3(s+c) = 2904
s+c = 2904÷3
→ s + c = 968

Find c (the price of cufflinks)
Using elimination :
→ s + c = 968
→ s - c =    82
------------------- -
...... 2c = 886
c = 886/2
c = $443
A pair of cufflinks costs $443
