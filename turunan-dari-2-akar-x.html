20. turunan pertama x akar x-2?

Maka,
[tex]$\begin{align}y'&=g'(x)h(x)+g(x)h'(x) \\ &=1.\sqrt{x-2}+x\times\frac{1}{2\sqrt{x-2}} \\ &=\sqrt{x-2}+\frac{x}{2\sqrt{x-2}} \\ &=\frac{2(x-2)+x}{2\sqrt{x-2}}=\frac{2x-4+x}{2\sqrt{x-2}} \\ &=\frac{3x-4}{2\sqrt{x-2}}\end{align}[/tex]

28. turunan dari y= cot (2 / akar x)


Y = cot (2 / akar x) = 1/tan(2/ akar x) = cos (2/akar x)/sin (2/akar x)
u = cos (2/akar x) ; v = sin (2/akar x)
u' = 1/x^(3/2)sin (2/akar x) ; v' = -1/x^(3/2)cos (2/akar x)
Y' = (u'v - uv') / v^2
= [1/x^(3/2)sin (2/akar x).sin (2/akar x) - cos(2/akar x).-1/x^(3/2)cos (2/akar x)]/[sin (2/akar x)]^2
= [1/x^(3/2)sin^2(2/akar x) + 1/x^(3/2)cos^2(2/akar x)]/[sin^2(2/akar x)]
= [1/x^(3/2)]/[sin^2(2/akar x)]
= 1/x^(3/2). csc^2(2/akar x)
