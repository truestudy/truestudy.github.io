16. konversi satuan dari 9m/s=…km/jam​


Jawaban:

1m = 1 ÷ 1000 = 1/1000km

1s = 1/3600s atau 1/jam

jadi 9m/s =

[tex]9 \times \frac{1}{1000} \div \frac{1}{3600} \\ = 9 \times \frac{1}{1000} \times 3600 \\ = 9 \times \frac{3600}{1000} \\ = 9 \times \frac{18}{5} \\ = \frac{162}{5} \\ = 32.4[/tex]

jadi hasilnya 32,4 km/jam
