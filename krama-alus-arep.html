4. Bapak ngomong yen mengko bengi arep ndhelok bal-balan . - Ngoko Alus : - Krama lugu : - Krama alus :


Bapak ngomong yen mengko bengi arep ndhelok bal-balan .

- Ngoko Alus : bapak ngendikan yen mengko bengi arep diutus ningali bal balan

- Krama lugu : bapak ngendikan menawi mangke dalu badhe diutus ningali bal balan

krama alus : bapak ngendikan menawi mangke dalu badhe diutus mirsani bal balan

5. Pak Bambang arep tindak kantor= Ngoko lugu , ngoko alus, krama lugu ,krama alus


Ngoko lugu : Pak Bambang arep lunga menyang kantor

Ngoko Alus : Pak Bambang arep tindhak kantor

Krama lugu : Pak Bambang badhe kesah ten kantor

Krama Alus : Pak Bambang badhe tindak dhateng kantor
