9. apa arti dari national awakening day


hari kebangkitan nasional (bahasa), is a teem for the period in the first half on the 20th century, during which people from many parts of the archipelago first began to develop a national consciousness as "Indonesians"The Indonesian National Awakening is a term for the period in the first half of the 20th century, during which people from many parts of the archipelago first began to develop a national consciousness as "Indonesians".

Artinya:
Kebangkitan Nasional Indonesia adalah istilah untuk periode di paruh pertama abad ke-20, di mana orang-orang dari berbagai bagian kepulauan pertama mulai mengembangkan kesadaran nasional sebagai "orang Indonesia".
