9. rangkumlah materi ungkapan greeting, parting,Dan apologizing​


Greeting adalah ekspresi yang menunjukkan penyapaan, cara menyapa dan menyambut seseorang. Contoh: Good morning, Good night, etc.Parting adalah ekspresi yang menunjukkan perpisahan, berpisah dengan sesuatu/seseorang. Contoh: Bye, Goodbye, See you again, etc.Apologizing adalah ekspresi yang menunjukkan permintaan maaf, cara meminta maaf terhadap seseorang. Contoh: Sorry, I am sorry, etc.

13. buatlah percakapan antara 2 orang tentang greeting dan partingbuat 1 percakapan greeting1 lagi percakapan parting.tolong di jawab ya kak abis maghrib di kumpulkan TwT​


Ketika bertemu seseorang, hal pertama yang dilakukan adalah menyapa atau greeting.

Setelah itu ngobrol sebentar, dimulai dengan basa-basi, dilanjutkan dengan ngobrol serius.

Walaupun tidak harus didahului dengan ngobrol serius, percakapan selanjutnya diakhiri dengan salam perpisahaan atau farewell atau leave taking atau parting.

Berikut adalah beberapa contoh percakapan bahasa inggris greeting & farewell / leave taking / parting. Percakapan berikut diambil dari buku DIALOGS FOR EVERYDAY USE (Short Situational Dialogs for Students of English as a Foreign Language) by JULIA M. DOBSON,DEAN CURRY

DIALOG 2

Formal Greetings and Farewell

Paul: Hello. How are you?

Don: Fine, thank you. How are you?

Paul: Fine, thanks. (bus sound­effect) Oh, excuse me—here’s my bus. Good­bye.

Don: Good­bye.

DIALOG 3

Informal Greetings and Farewells

Dick: Hi! How are you?

Helen: Fine, thanks—and you?

Dick: Just fine. Where are you going?

Helen: To the library. Dick: O.K. I’ll see you later. So long

Helen: So long.

DIALOG 4

Informal Greetings and Farewells

JANE: Hi, Helen! How’s it going?

HELEN: Fine, thanks — and you?

JANE: Just fine. Where are you off to?

HELEN: To the library. I’ve got a history exam next week and need to start studying. Ugh.

JANE: Oh, no. Well, I’ll see you later then. Good luck!

HELEN: Thanks. See you later

DIALOG 5

Formal Greetings

JAMES: Good morning, Professor Austin, how are you doing?

PROFESSOR AUSTIN: Good morning, James. I am doing well. And you?

JAMES: I’m great, thank you. This is my friend Emma. She is thinking about applying to this college. She has a few questions. Would you mind telling us about the process, please?

PROFESSOR AUSTIN: Hello, Emma! It’s a pleasure to meet you. I’m more than happy to speak with you. Please stop by my office next week.

EMMA: It’s a pleasure to meet you, professor. Thank you so much for helping us.

PROFESSOR AUSTIN: Don’t mention it. Hopefully, I will be able to answer your questions!

28. contoh dialog bhs inggris menggunakan kalimat greeting,parting,gratitude,and aplogy​


Jawaban:

Pembahasan:

Sebelum membuat dialog, kita harus mengetahui bagaimana kalimat yang dapat digunakan untuk mengekpresikan greeting, parting, thanking, dan apologizing. Berikut contohnya.

1. expressing greeting

- hello

- hi

- good morning/ afternoon/ evening

2. expressing parting

- good bye

- see you later

- see you

3. expressing thanking

- thank you

- thank you very much

- thanks for ...

4. Expressing apologizing

- i'm sorry

- forgive me

- i do apologize

berikut contoh dialognya:

A: Hey, guys!

B, C, and E : Hey, A!

A: Where is D?

C: You know, he is never on time

B: But the movie will start in 20 minutes

E: i have called him. He is on the way.

A: Let's wait for him

*15 minutes later*

D: Hey, guys. I'm so sorry for being late.

B: You never change

C: Luckily, you are here before the movie starts so we still wait for you

D: Thanks for waiting for me

A: It's alright. Let's go to the theater

*after watching the movie*

C: the movie was so great!

B: Yeah, i like when the actor escapes from the terrorists.

E: Guys, i'm sorry i have to go now. I have to pick my father up at the airport

A: That's okay, E.

D: yeah, it's okay.

E: Alright. Good bye, guys! let's hang out again.

A, B, C, and D: Bye, E.
