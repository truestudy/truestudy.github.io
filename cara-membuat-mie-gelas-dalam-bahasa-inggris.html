400 ml water

add the leaves and noodles in the boiled water until soft , and the noodles is ready to put in the bowl.

8. cara membuat mie goreng dalam bahasa inggris?


ingredients

Noodles
Onions
Garlic
Olive oil
Tomatoes
Lemoncarrots

Steps

1. Prepare all the necessary ingredients on the table or another flat surface. Make sure you have everything you need. Chop the onions, garlic, carrots, and tomatoes on the cutting board. Cut the ingredients into very small cube-like pieces so the noodles will get the taste of every ingredient.


2. Heat your frying pan and add some olive oil in it while it's heating up.


3. Open the noodles from the package and crumble them into average pieces (not too small though). Smaller pieces have a higher chance of cooking all the way through.


4. Your crumbled noodles should look a bit small like you see in the picture.


5. After your frying pan is hot enough with the oil and such, just put all of the chopped ingredients into the frying pan.Leave it there for a while and stir.


6. If the chopped ingredients are ready after some time, add the crumbled noodles there. Stir it for a while.


7. If the noodles smell great with the others, add the seasoning that came with the packet. If you didn't get one, then just add a pinch a salt or more according to your taste. Stir it up again. Now add some water in it. The noodle shouldn't sink in the water. Only 75 % of it should be.


8. Keep out creation in the frying pan until all the water soaks up in the noodles.Now, squeeze about half a lime or according to your taste. There you have it! You're now ready to serve the president some nice fried noodles.

28. Cara membuat mie goreng dalam bahasa inggris


Ingredients
– 400 grams of wet noodles, pour with hot water, drained
– 10 pieces of fish meatballs, beef meatballs, sliced ​​thin
– 4 eggs whipped, add a little salt
– 2 leaves of spring onion, finely sliced
– 1 carrot, sliced ​​matchsticks
– 5 spring onions, mixed
– 3 cloves garlic, mixed
– 2 tablespoons sweet soy sauce
– 1 tablespoon salt soy sauce
– salt as needed
– pepper as needed
– 1 teaspoon of chicken powder
– 125 grams of sprouts
– vegetable oil as needed
– fried onions as needed for topping

How To Make Fried Noodles
– Fry two eggs, roll it, slice it finely, set aside.
– Heat 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil, add the other eggs, stir and set aside.
– Then heat 3 tablespoons of vegetable oil, add meatballs, stir it well.
– Then put onion and garlic, stir evenly, add scrambled egg, put seasoning ingredients such as soy sauce, pepper, salt, etc as you need.
– For the last, add the other ingredients such as carrots, chicken powder, sprouts, and stir it evenly, then set aside.
– The fried noodles is ready to serve.
