6. susunlah kalimat berikut am - I - library - in the - now - reading​


Susunannya adalah “I am reading in the library now”

PEMBAHASAN

PRESENT CONTINUOUS digunakan untuk menyatakan Kegiatan yang sedang berlangsung saat ini.

Adverb of time => now ✔️=> Right now=> at the moment=> today

Rumus Present Continuous

(+) S + is/am/are + V-ing + O/C + Adv

(-) S + is/am/are + not + V-ing + O/C + Adv

(?) is/am/are + S + V-ing + O/C + Adv?

CatatanI + am ✔️He/she/it + isYou/we/they + are

KESIMPULAN

I am reading in the library now

➡️ Saya sedang membaca Di perpustakaan sekarang

Keterangan:Subject => IBe => amVerb-ing => Reading Complement => in the library Adverb of time => now

answer

correct answer is d. has never been performed

whay:

because the sentence is referring to a past action the performance of the play that never happened. therefore use the present perfect tense has never been performed to indicate that the action was not completed at any point in the past up until the present moment.
