Materi tentang Asking and Giving Opinion

Asking : meminta pendapat

Giving : memberikan pendapat

Some Expressions of Asking Opinion :

• What do you think of/ about......?

• What is your opinion on/ about....?

• Can you give me your opinion on/about....?

• Etc.

Some Expressions of giving Opinion :

• I think.....

• To me.....

• In my opinion,.....

• Etc.
