2. After that, fold the wide end underneath the narrow end.

3. And then, pass the wide end horizontally over the narrow end again.

4. Next, take the wide end up and through the loop around your neck.

5. After that, take the wide end through the knot in front.

6.Last but not least, proceed to tighten the knot and pull it up to your collar.

3. cara memakai dasi dalam bahasa inggris 10


1. Pull the tie around the neck, the position of the tip of the width of the tie (fuller) should be longer than the thinner end and cross, where the fuller part is above the thinner.
2. Rotate the width towards the back just below the thin part of the tie.
3. Pull back and rotate the tie width towards the front again.
4. Pull the width up, input into the circle between the shirt and tie.
5. Hold and hold the front of the knot with your thumb and bring the width downward through the front circle.
6. Move your finger, and tighten the knot with the curved shape to the collar part of the shirt by holding the thin part of the tie and pull the knot to be cleaned.

22. prosedur cara memakai dasi dlm bahasa inggris


How to tie a simple knot

steps:
1. Start with the backside of the tie facing away from you, the wide end is on the right and the small end is on the left. The tip of the small end should rest slightly above your belly-button. Move only the active (wide) end.
2. Wide end under the small end to the left.
3. Across the small end to the right.
4. Up into the neck loop from underneath.
5. Down through the loop you've just made in the front.
6. Pull down on the wide end to tighten. Slide the knot up to adjust.
