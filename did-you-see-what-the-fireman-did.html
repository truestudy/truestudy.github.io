7. If the fireman hadnt arrived earlier, they would not saved the house change the statement into conditional type 1 and type 2


MAPEL : BAHASA INGGRIS

MATERI : CONDITIONAL SENTENCE

KELAS : 9

KODE SOAL : 9.5

KODE KATEGORISASI : 9.5.2


SOAL DAN PEMBAHASAN

==========================

If the fireman hadnt arrived earlier, they would not have saved the house.


The conditional type 1 and 2 are :


Sebelum kita menjawab kalimat di atas, berikut adalah rumus conditional type 1 and 2

1). TYPE 1

if + Simple Present , will-Future


2). TYPE 2

if + Simple Past , Conditional I (= would + Infinitive)


So, the answers are :

If the fireman hadn't arrived earlier, they would not have saved the house.


Type 1 : If the fireman doesn't arrive earlier, they will not save the house.


Type 2 : If the fireman didn't arrive earlier, they would not save the house.
