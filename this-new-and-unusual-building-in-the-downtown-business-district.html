District is the division of administrative areas in the provinces of Papua and West Papua, Indonesia, under districts or cities. The term "district" replaces "kecamatan" which was previously used as in other provinces in Indonesia. District is an apparatus of a Regency or City in Papua which has a certain working area led by a District Head.

Districts are further divided into a number of villages, or under other names according to local customs. The formation, expansion, abolition, or merger of districts is stipulated by a Regency or City Regulation.

28. (+) the governMent Built a New school Building in my Town last Year


(+) The government built a new school building in my town last year.

(-) The government didn't build a new school building in my town last year.

(?) Did the government build a new school building in my town last year?

PEMBAHASAN:

Simple Past Tense

↪adalah pola kalimat dalam Bahasa Inggris yang membicarakan tentang suatu hal atau aktifitas yang telah dilakukan pada masa lampau.

➡Rumus:

(+) Subject + Verb 2 + Object

(-) Subject + Did + Not + Verb 1 + Object

(?) Did + Subject + Verb 1 + Object

➡Contoh kalimat:

(+) Yayan ate fried rice yesterday

(-) Yayan didn't eat fried rice yesterday

(?) Did Yayan eat fried rice yesterday?

➡Keterangan:Did not dapat disingkat menjadi didn't.Pada kalimat negatif past tense kita sudah tidak menggunakan Verb 2 namun yang kita gunakan adalab Verb 1 pada kalimatnya.

➡Time Space:

- Yesterday

- Last year

- A week ago

- Dan lain-lain
