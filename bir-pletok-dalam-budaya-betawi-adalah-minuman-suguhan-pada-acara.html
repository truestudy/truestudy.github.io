22. 3. Why do Betawi people make bir pletok? 4. Why is it called bir pletok? 5. What are the benefits of drinking bir pletok?​


answer:

3.The Dutch who once occupied Indonesia, have a tradition in every celebration of their events. They always serve beer as a signature dish to celebrate something. The beer drunk by the Dutch is a beer that contains alcohol. If you drink it in large quantities it can be intoxicating.

The Betawi people who saw the habits of the Dutch became interested and wanted to try the typical Western drink. However, most of the Betawi people follow Islam and do not consume alcohol. Therefore, the Betawi people make special drinks such as beer but not alcoholic.

4.The name "pletok" itself is taken from the sound when a beer lid made of wood is opened, it will produce a "pletok" or "plup" sound. Therefore, the Betawi people named the special drink "Pletok Beer". Beer from Europe (West) serves to warm the body.

5.Pletok beer is used to warm the body, prevent colds and improve blood circulation

30. tuliskan cara membuat bir pletok!Menurutmu, apakah kita harus melestarikan minuman bir pletok? jelaskan!​


Bahan-bahan

800 ml air

4 lmbr daun pandan, cuci bersih, buat simpul

4 lmbr daun jeruk

4 cm kayu manis, cuci bersih

1/2 biji pala, cuci bersih

3 bh kapulaga bundar, belah tidak putus, cuci bersih

4 bh cengkeh, cuci bersih

150 gr gula pasir, bisa ditambah jika kurang manis

3 bh sereh, geprek bagian putihnya, cuci bersih

50 gr kayu secang, cuci bersih

Secukupnya es batu

Langkah

Siapkan semua bahan.

Rebus air, masukkan kapulaga, kayu manis, sereh, jahe & biji pala

Sobek sobek daun jeruk kemudian masukkan kedalam rebusan bersama daun pandan, cengkeh dan gula pasir. Aduk aduk dan cek rasa, jika kurang manis bisa ditambahkan sesuai selera.

Masukkan kayu secang. Aduk kembali sampai warna air berubah jadi merah. Tutup panci dan masak sekitar 20mnt dengan api kecil sampai airnya sedikit surut. Matikan api.

Siapkan botol, tumbler atau shaker.

Letakkan secukupnya es batu di dalamnya. Kemudian saring rebusan rempah tersebut kedalam tumbler yang berisi es batu.

Tutup rapat tumbler kemudian di guncang guncangkan sampai keluar busa/buih.

Letakkan es batu dalam gelas saji. Kemudian tuang bir pletok yang sudah dikocok tadi. Sajikan.

Harus, agar bir pletok tidak akan tertinggal zaman dan kita bisa melestarikannya dengan cara membuka usaha minuman bur pletok ataupun mempelajari resep dan cara memasaknya
