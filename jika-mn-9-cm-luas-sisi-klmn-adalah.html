[tex]l = \frac{(kl + mn) \times kn}{2} \\ 105 = \frac{(9x + x) \times 7}{2} \\ 105 \times 2 = 10x \times 7 \\ 210= 70x \\ x = 3[/tex]

kl=9*3=27

mn=1*3=3

selisih kl-mn(lo)=27-3=24

sisi miring =

[tex] \sqrt{ {24}^{2} + {7}^{2} } \\ = \sqrt{576 + 49} \\ = \sqrt{625} \\ = 25[/tex]

keliling =kl+mn+kn+miring

=27+3+7+25=62 cm
