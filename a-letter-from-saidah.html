surat dari saidah

saya baru menerjemahkannya pada translate

Jawaban:

Assalamualaikum Alia,

Sangat menyenangkan membaca suratmu tentang dirimu dan kampung halamanmu.

Aku sangat ingin menjadi sahabat penamu.

Aku seorang siswi dari Johor Baru di Malaysia.

Sebenarnya aku siswi dari pesantren di luar kota, tapi keluargaku tinggal di Kuala Lumpur. Kakak perempuan tertuaku adalah dokter. Dia akan segera menikah.

Adik laki2ku adalah seorang siswa SD di Kuala Lumpur tapi dia sering menulis surat untukku lewat e-mail.

Pelajaran favoritku adalah IPS. Aku paling suka sejarah; itu membantuku tahu lebih banyak tentang pebedaan negara2 pada masa lampau.

Di sekolah, kami disuruh menggunakan bahasa inggris setiap saat, bahkan saat kita ada di asrama, jadi kita menjadi sedikit lancar dalam bahasa Inggris meskipun terkadang kami tergelincir berbicara bahasa Malay (melayu), dimana itu adalah bahasa ibu kami.

Untuk hobiku, aku sangat suka pada lagu2 dan musik. Penyanyi favoritku adalah Yusuf Islam yang mana sebelumnya dia bernama Cat Steven. Dia sangat keren.

Penyaynyi lain yang kusuka adalah Maher Zain dengan lagu2 religinya. Penyanyi Malaysia favoritku tentu saja Siti Nurhaliza.

Aku juga suka menonton film, yerutama komedi. Aktor yang paling kusuka adalah Tom Cruise.

Aku sangat suka buku. Aku suka membaca novel dan cerita2 pendek, rata2 yang dibuat oleh pengarang Malaysia yang mungkin kamu tidak bakal tahu. Aku suka beberapa pengarang berbahasa inggris, seperti J.K. Rowling dan juga pengarang2 Indonesia seperti Andtea Hirata dan Ahmad Fuadi.

Cita2ku, ketika aku besar nanti, adalah menjadi pengarang buku sains-fiksi yang berlatar pada masa depan yang sangat jauh.

Aku ingin sekali pergi ke Indonesia suatu hari nanti. Aku dengar Indonesia adalah negara muslim terbesar.

Buku yang baru aku baca tadi menyebutkan kalau ada beberapa tempat yang sangat menarik untuk dikunjungi, seperti, Bali, Sulawesi, Papua, dan Kalimantan!

Bagaimana denganmu, apakah kamu mau mengunjungi negaraku?

Wassalam, Saidah

Bersemangatlah,

Saidah

18. main idea a letter from saidah


kelas : X
pelajaran : Bahasa Inggris
kategori : Taking about self
kata kunci : letter from Saidah, Main idea, detail

pembahasan:
paragraf 1, main idea dan detail sudah ada di buku.

paragraf 2
I'm a sixteen year old school student from Johor Bahru in Malaysia. Actually I attend an Islamic boarding school just outside the city but my family live in Kuala Lumpur. My eldest sister is a medical doctor. She will get married soon. My younger brother is an elementary school student in KL but he often writes to me via email.
main idea : Saidah tells Alia about her school and her family.
details:
1) She is a sixteen year old school student from Johor Bahru in Malaysia.
2) She attends an Islamic boarding school just outside the city
3) Her family live in Kuala Lumpur.
4) Her eldest sister is a medical doctor and will get married soon.
5) Her younger brother is an elementary school student in KL but he often writes to her via email.

paragraf 3
My favorite subjects are social sciences. I like history very much, it helps me know more how different countries existed in the past. At school we are supposed to use English at all times, even when we are in the dormitory, so we have become quite fluent although sometimes we slip back into Malay, which is our mother tongue.
main idea : Saidah tells Alia about her favorite subjects.
details:
1) Her favorite subjects are social sciences.
2) She likes history very much
3) They supposed to use English at all times.

paragraf 4
As for hobbies, I'm really into songs and music. My favorite singer is Yusuf Islam whose former name was Cat Steven. He's so cool! Another singer I like is Maher Zain with his religious songs. My favorite Malay singer is of course Siti Nurhaliza. I also like watching movies, especially comedies. The actor i like best is Tom Cruise.
main idea : Saidah tells Alia about her hobbies and her favorite singers and actor.
details :
1) She is really into songs and music
2) Her favorite singer is Yusuf Islam, Maher Zain, and Siti Nurhaliza.
3) She also likes watching movies.
4) The actor she likes best is Tom Cruise.

paragraf 5
I'm really into books. I like reading novels and short stories. Mostly by Malay authors who you probably haven't heard of. I like some writers in English, like JK Rowling and Indonesian writers too, like Andrean Hirata and Ahmad Fuadi. My dream, when I'm older, is to be a writer of science fiction books set in the distant future.
main idea : Saidah tells Alia about her reading hobby.
details :
1) She is really into books.
2) She likes reading novels and short stories by Malay.
3) She likes some writers in English, like JK Rowling and Indonesian writers too, like Andrean Hirata and Ahmad Fuadi.
4) Her dream when she is older is to be a writer of science fiction books set in the distant future.

paragraf 6
I'd really love to come to Indonesia some day. I heard that it has the largest number of Muslims of any country. A book that I've just read mentions that there are some magnificent places to visit, such as, Bali, Sulawesi, Papua and Borneo. What about you, do you want to visit my country? Wassalam, Saidah
main idea : Saidah tells Alia about her interest to visit Indonesia
details:
1) She heard that it has the largest number of Muslims of any country.
2) She just reads a book which mentions that there are some magnificent places to visit, such as, Bali, Sulawesi, Papua and Borneo.
