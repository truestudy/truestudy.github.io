19. Which one is true? 1) Michelle going to begin medical school next year, he wants to be a doctor Or 2) Michelle is going to begin medical school next year, he wants to be a doctor And—why?


The true sentence ia 2) Michelle is going to begin medical school next year, he wants to be a doctor.

Because the tenses is used in this sentence is simple future tense. The sentence above express a prior plan so the pattern is:

▪️Subject + be going to + verb1.

✔️ The subject is Michelle—Michelle is a singular noun so to be is "is".

Pembahasan

Simple Future Tense is a tense to show the time in the future. Time signal:

TomorrowLetterTonightNext year, etc.

Pattern

(+) Subject + will/be going to + verb1.

(-) Subject + will not/be not going to + verb1.

(?) Will + subject + verb1? or Be + subject + going to + verb1?

notes:

Be => am/is/areAm => IIs => he, she, it, singular noun.Are => you, we, they, plural noun.

The Difference function between Will and Be going to

1️⃣To express prediction : Either will and Be going to used.

example:

You are the first rank in the class, You Will win on the science olympiade.You are the first rank in the class, You are be going to win on the science olympiade.

2️⃣To express a prior plan : Be Going to

example:

Tonight I am going to study hard to get the high score.

3️⃣To express willingness : Will

example:

Will you help me to bring these boxes?
