Days of the week:

Sunday: MingguMonday: SeninTuesday: SelasaWednesday: RabuThursday: KamisFriday: JumatSaturday: Sabtu

Vocabulary used in the days of the week:

Today: hari iniYesterday: kemarinTomorrow: besok2 days ago: 2 hari lalu2 days later: lusaThe day after tomorrow: lusaBefore: sebelumAfter: sesudah

Example:

Today is Monday. Yesterday is Sunday.

Today is Wuesday. Tomorrow will be Wednesday.

The day after friday is Saturday.

The day before Tuesday is Monday

This month : bulan iniLast month: bulan lalu2 months ago: 2 bulan laluNext month: bulan depan3 months later: 3 bulan kemudian

Example:

Next month is November.

Last month was September.
