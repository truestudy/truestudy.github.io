Volume (Satuan Liter)

Cara :

[tex]debit = \frac{volume}{waktu} \\ \\ 200 \: \frac{cc}{detik} = \frac{volume}{20 \: menit} \\ \\ 200 \: \frac{cc}{detik} = \frac{volume}{20 \times 60} \\ \\ 200 \: \frac{cc}{detik} = \frac{volume}{1.200 \: detik} \\ \\ 20 0 \times 1.200 \: cc = volume \\ \\ 240.000 \: cc = volume \\ \\ \frac{240.000}{1.000} \: liter = volume \\ \\ 240 \: liter = volume[/tex]

1) Sistem kubik

2) Sistem liter
