14. Have just moved into a new house artinya

I have just moved into a new house = aku baru saja pindah ke sebuah rumah baru.

____

vocabulary:

I = aku

have just = baru saja

moved = pindah

into = ke

a = sebuah

new = baru

house = rumah

____

simple present perfect: digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu peristiwa yang sudah terjadi. biasanya ditandai dengan JUST, YET, SINCE. Rumusnya:

untuk kalimat verbal:

subject + have/has + verb 3

kalimat nominal:

subject + have/has + been + adjective/adverb/noun


15. jamitra and yosephine(move).... into new house for a week a. moves b. moving c. moved d. has moved e. have moved


Jamitra and yosephine have moved into new house for a week

Jawabannya yang E. ✔️

PEMBAHASAN Present Perfect

Digunakan untuk menyatakan Kegiatan atau Peristiwa yg sudah selesai tapi masih ada kaitannya di masa sekarang.

Ciri Present Perfect => For=> Since => Already => dll.

Rumus Present Perfect(+) S + has/have + V3 (-) S + has/have + not + V3 (?) has/have + S + V3?

Catatan

➡️ I/you/we/they/2 nama + have✔️

➡️ He/she/it/satu nama + has

Pilihan yg tersedia:a. moves ➡️ Verb+sb. moving ➡️ Verb-ingc. moved ➡️ Verb-2d. has moved ➡️ has + V3e. have moved = have + V3 ✔️

KESIMPULAN

Jamitra and Yosephine have moved into new house for a week

Ada 2 nama Ada ciri Present Perfect “for”

Jadi, menggunakan Have + V3 = have moved (E) ✔️
