2. how to make a sekoteng iced


Ingredients


100 grams of pomegranate seeds (Chinese girlfriend.)

200 grams peanuts, boil and discard husk.

100 grams green beans, boiled.

1000 cc of water.

100 grams of sugar.

1 knuckle of ginger.

5 pieces of bread, a small slice like dice.


How To Make

Boil some pomegranate seeds or chinese girlfriend until soft, remove and drain.

Mix water, sugar and ginger. Cook until boiling. Lift, clean filter.

Sekoteng Serve in bowls. Give 2 tablespoons pomegranate seeds, 2 tablespoons peanut and green, and pieces of bread. Then flush with water ginger / wedang ginger has been cooked.

9. How to make iced lemon


How to make iced lemon :

1) Bring water to boil until it bubbles, then add the tea and boil in medium flame for 3 minutes.
2) Filter through a tea strainer and add sugar, mix to dissolve it. Cool down to room temperature.
3) Squeeze lemon in the tea. Fill a tall glass with ice and pour the tea over it and enjoy! Using a vegetable peeler, remove bright yellow peel from lemons. Combine lemon peel and sugar in a medium saucepan with 1/2 cup water and bring to a boil, stirring to dissolve sugar. Lower heat and simmer 2 minutes.

Steep the tea bags in 4 cups of boiling water, covered, for 5 minutes. Then remove tea bags.
