27. Look at the pictures. What may the people in the pictures say? Perform the dialogue ​


Perform the dialogue.

Picture No.1

A dialog between parents (a father and a mother) and their son, Andi.

Mother: “Good morning, Andi, did you have a good sleep last night?”

Andi: “Yes, I did, Mother”.

Father: “We are going to Ancol today for sight seeing, are you ready?”

Andi: “Yes, of course, I am ready! I want to swim on the beach!”

Picture No. 2

A dialog between Joni and Riki when they met after school.

Joni: “Hi, Riki, how are you doing? You look happy!”

Riki: “Hi, Joni, I am fine, thanks. Yes, I have to go back home soon because my mother is taking me to a movie tonight”.

Joni: “Oh, that’s nice. Have fun and enjoy your time with your family”.

Riki: “Ok, thanks, Joni. You, too, have a nice day, say hi to your family, also”.

Joni: “Ok sure, bye!”

Picture No. 3

A dialog between Rudi and his school teacher Pak Djoko.

Pak Djoko: “Hi, Rudi, how are you?”

Rudi: “I’m fine, Pak Djoko, thanks”.

Pak Djoko: “By the way, you did very well in your exam yesterday, you got an A”.

Rudi: “Oh, thank you for letting me know, Pak Djoko”.

Pak Djoko: “You must have studied well”.

Rudi: “Yes, Pak Djoko, I studied very hard for the exam”.

Pak Djoko: “Good for you. Keep up the good work!”’

Rudi: “Thanks, Pak Djoko, have a nice day!”

Picture No. 4

A dialog between Dono and his neighboor (a husband and wife, Toni and Susi).

Susi: “Hi, Dono, what are you doing? You look so happy!”

Dono: “Hi, Ibu Susi, I am happy because today is my birthday and my parents will give me a present tonight”.

Toni: “Oh, Happy Birthday to you”.

Susi: “Yeah, Happy Bday to you, Dono, how old are you now?”

Dono: “I’m 10 years old Ibu Susi”.

Susi: “Well, Ok, have fun for your birthday with your family!”

Dono: “Ok, thanks, I will”.

Pembahasan

Dialog merupakan suatu perbincangan antara dua atau lebih pihak yang saling berinteraksi tentang sesuatu topik pembicaraan. Dialog umumnya memakai bahasa sehari-hari (non-formal) yang menggunakan Simple Present Tense.

Ciri-ciri sebuah Dialog adalah:

Pembicaraan membahas tentang suatu pokok perbincangan.Pihak-pihak yang berinteraksi biasanya menggunakan bahasa yang dapat dimengerti satu sama lain.Penulisan Dialog pada umumnya diawali dengan mencantumkan nama pembicara langsung.Kalimat Dialog selalu diawali/ditutup dengan tanda kutip (“...“).
