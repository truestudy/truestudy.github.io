22. USING PAST PERFECT TENSE. contohEXAMPLE :  I TOOK A BATH AT 4 PM YESTERDAY.                     MY FRIEND CAME TO MY HOUSE AT 6 PM YESTERDAY.                      I HAD TAKEN A BATH WHEN MY FRIEND CAME TO MY HOUSE YESTERDAY. 1.       WE GOT HOME LATE PM LAST NIGHT.THE CHILDREN FELL ASLEEP AT 9 PM LAST NIGHT. 2.       THE FLOOD WASHED AWAY THE BRIDGE.WE CROSSED THE RIVER. 3.       I READ THE NEWSPAPER AT 10 AM YESTERDAY.THE RAIN STOPPED AT 9 AM YESTERDAY. 4.        SHE WENT TO THE MALL IN THE MORNING.HER MOTHER ARRIVED AT 12 PM. 5.       HE WAS A NEWSPAPER REPORTER IN 2019.HE WAS A DOCTOR IN 2018. bantuin dong yang jawab jadikan terbaik lah ​


Jawaban:

1.  The children had felt asleep when we got home last night

2.  We had crossed the river when the flood washed away the bridge

3.  The rain had stopped when I read the newspaper yesterday

4.  She had gone to the mall when her mother arrived

Jawaban:

1. We had gotten home late when the children fell asleep last night.

2. The flood had washed away the bridge when we crossed the river.

3. I had read the newspaper when the rain stopped yesterday.

4. She had gone to the mall when her mom arrived.

5. He had been a newspaper reporter when he was a doctor.

Penjelasan:

Past perfect Tense menerangkan suatu perbuatan yang sudah selesai dilakukan pada waktu lampau, atau menjelaskan dua peristiwa yang telah terjadi, tetapi menegaskan peristiwa mana yg terlebih dahulu terjadi. Seperti contoh di soal nomor 1:

-We had gotten home late when the children fell asleep last night. (kami sudah datang terlambat, ketika anak-anak tidur tadi malam.)

Ket: kami sudah datang terlambat (kejadian yg lebih dulu terjadi), anak-anak tidur tadi malam (kejadian yg terjadi setelah peristiwa pertama terjadi).

Jadi, perbuatan pertama yg selesai kita gunakan Past perfect, perbuatan yg kedua kita gunakan Simple Past Tense.
