Jawaban:

1. Homework wasn't done by the students yesterday. (A)

2. Grandma is often helped by his grandson, Brian. (A)

3. The car will be washed this afternoon. (C)

4. The morning newspaper hasn't been read yet. (B)

5. A snapshot has been taken by my father. (B)

6. Has the phone been answered by you when I call? (D)

7. The park will be cleaned next week. (B)

8. More and more trees are cut down every year. (A)

9. Cartoons are watched by the kids every evening (D)

10. Stamps were collected by my dad when he was a boy. (D)

Penjelasan:

1. Perhatikan kata "yesterday".

Kata "yesterday" menunjukkan bahwa kalimat tersebut menggunakan passive simple past tense (was/were + verb 3).

2. Kalimat ini menggunakan simple present tense (am/is/are + adverb of frequency + verb 3).

3. Kalimat ini menggunakan present future tense (will + be + verb 3).

4. Perhatikan kata "yet" yang biasanya digunakan pada negative present perfect tense (have/hasn't + been + verb 3 + yet).

5. Kalimat ini menggunakan present perfect tense (have/has + been + verb 3).

6. Perhatikan kalimat "when I call" yang menggunakan simple present tense. Ini menunjukkan kalimat sebelumnya present perfect tense.

7. Kalimat ini menggunakan present future tense (will + be + verb 3) karena terdapat keterangan waktu "next week".

8. Kalimat ini menggunakan simple present tense (am/is/are + verb 3) karena terdapat keterangan waktu "every year".

9. Kalimat ini menggunakan simple present tense (am/is/are + verb 3) karena terdapat keterangan waktu "every evening".

10. Perhatikan kalimat "when he was a boy" yang menunjukkan waktu lampau.
