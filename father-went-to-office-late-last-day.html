5. Change this sentences into negative She went to bandung last day A. she don`t go to bandung last day B. She doesn`t go to bandung last day C. She didn`t go to bandung last day D. She wouldn`t go to bandung last day Bantuin jawab


Jawaban:

C. She didn't go to bandung last day.

Penjelasan:

Negative sentences are declarative statements. That is, they relay information believed to be true. Negative sentences are typically formed by adding the word "not" after the helping verb. The most popular helping verbs are a form of "to be," including "am," "is," "are," "was" and "were."

Example:

I am not flying to England.

He wasn't eating white rice.

We were not sad when he moved away.

He doesn't have to commute to work.

They will not be joining us for dinner tonight.

They weren't playing poker.

17. (+) My father went to the Bali last week.​


Jawaban:

(-)My father didnt go to Bali last week

Penjelasan:

Why? Because the pattern of verbal simple past tense

(+) Subject + verb 2 + Time Signals

(-) Subject didnt + verb 1 + Time Signals
(?) Did + Subject + verb 1 + Time Signals

From the pattern the positive sentences is wearing verb 2 but if you are making negative or interrogative sentence the use verb 1
Hope you learn the lesson

19. 1. My father goes to office every day (+).........................................................(-).........................................................(?).bantu jawab titik nya ​


Jawaban:

( - ) My father does not go to office every day.

( ? ) Does my father go to office every day?

Topik :

Simple Present Tenses

Rumus :

VERBAL

(+) S + V1 + O

(-) S + Do Not + V1 + O

(?) Do/Does + S + V1 + O?

(+) You love her

(-) You do not love her

(?) Do you lover her?

NOMINAL

(+) S + Is/Am/Are + 3C

(-) S + Is/Am/Are + Not + 3C

(?) Is/Am/Are + S + 3C?

Time signal :

- Today, everyday, everynight, on sunday, in april, at 5 o'clock, etc.

- Always, usually, often, sometimes, rarely, seldom, never

Catatan :

3C (Adjective, Noun, Adverb)

Penjelasan :

Simple Present Tenses adalah Tenses yang menyatakan hal hal yang berkaitan dengan fakta atau menyatakan aktifitas yg menjadi kebiasaan
