1. KPK 3 dan 5 = ?

3 = 3.

5 = 5.

KPK = 3 × 5 = 15.

2. KPK 10 dan 15 = ?

10 = 2 × 5.

15 = 3 × 5.

KPK = 5 × 2 × 3 = 30.

3. KPK 14 dan 21 = ?

14 = 2 × 7.

21 = 3 × 7.

KPK = 7 × 2 × 3 = 42.

4. KPK 20 dan 25 = ?

20 = 2² × 5.

25 = 5².

KPK = 2² × 5² = 4 × 25 = 100.
