13. 1.what is the text about?2.what happened to the partisipasnt?3.what made them not happy?4.if everything has been improve, what do you think will happen?5.what will the head master?


Jawaban:

Arti dari what is the text about adalah mengenai apa teks tersebut. Jawaban dari what is the text about adalah the text is about .... Dalam bahasa Indonesia berarti tek tersebut mengenai ....

Pembahasan

Untuk menjawab pertanyaan what is the text about, kita dapat menemukan jawabannya dari:

Judul bacaan.

Kalimat pertama pada paragraf pertama. Pada teks yang tidak menuliskan judul, tema terdapat pada kalimat pertama. Hal ini biasanya terdapat pada report text, descriptive text, biography text.

Kata kunci. Kata kunci biasanya dituliskan berulang-ulang pada teks tersebut.

Pertanyaan what is the text about biasanya terkait dengan tema atau ide pokok suatu bacaan. Sama halnya dalam bahasa Indonesia, tema atau ide pokok dapat ditemukan di judul, di kalimat pertama pada paragraf pertama, dan kata kunci.

20. she went to the situation... she had prepared everything she needed


The Answer : 
------------------

She went to the situation Because she Had prepared everything she needed

Past Tense + Before + Past Perfect tenseAnswer:

The exercise above seems to be about complex sentences and clause of connectors. From the two sentences provided, 'she went to the situation' and 'she had prepared everything she needed.', you can see the cause and effect relation between them. The first one seems to be the effect of the second one, roughly.
So, here's the answer:
She went to the situation because/ as/ since/ for she had prepared everything she needed.
