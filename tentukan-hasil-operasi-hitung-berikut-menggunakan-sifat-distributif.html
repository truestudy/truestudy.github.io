1. Tentukan hasil operasi hitung berikut menggunakan sifat distributif​


[tex]a. \: (15 \times 32) + (15 \times 18) = 15 \times (32 + 18) = 15 \times 50 = 750[/tex]

[tex]b. \: (8 \times 41) + (8 \times 59) = 8 \times (41 + 59) = 8 \times 100 = 800[/tex]

[tex]c. \: (27 \times 112) - (27 \times 12) = 27 \times (112 - 12) = 27 \times 100 = 2700[/tex]

[tex]d. \: (150 \times 32) - (150 \times 12) = 150 \times (32 - 12) = 150 \times 20 = 3000[/tex]

[tex]3 \times 483 = 3 \times (400 + 80 + 3) = (3 \times 400) + (3 \times 80) + (3 \times 3) = 1200 + 240 + 9 = 1449[/tex]
