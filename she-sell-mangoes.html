14. When Ginny buys 3 kg of mangoes, she will have $7 left. However, she will need another $13 if she buys 5 Kyodo mangoes. How much money does she have?


Jawaban:

$37

Penjelasan:

Bahasa Indonesia: Saat Ginny membeli 3 Kg mangga, ia memiliki sisah $7. Namun, ia akan memerlukan $13 lebih untuk membeli 5 Kg Mangga. Uang yang ia miliki adalah

Perhitungan :

Harga : 3 Kg = sisah $7

           : 5 Kg = +$13

Maka, melalui informasi itu kita ketahui

2 Kg = $13 + $7 = $20

1 Kg = $20 : 2

       = $10

Uang = Harga ( 5 Kg ) - Kekurangan

         = ( 5 Kg x $10 ) - $13

         = $37

21. Merry has $10. If she buys 8 pears and 5 mangoes, she will be short of $1.10. If she buys 5 pears and 4 mangoes, she will recieves $1.75 in change. Find the price of 1 pear and 1 mango?


[tex]p = pear \\ m = mango \\ \\ 8p + 5m = 10 + 1.1 \\ 8p + 5m = 11.1 \\ \\ 5p + 4m = 10 - 1.75 \\ 5p + 4m = 8.25 \\ 8p + 5m = 10 + 1.1 \\ \: \: 8p + 5m = 11.1 | \times 4 = 32p + 20m = 44.4 \\ \: \: \: 5p + 4m = \: \: 8.25 | \times 5 = 25p + 20m = 41.25 \\ .......................................................................... - \\ \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: 7p \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: = \: \: 3.15 \\ \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: p = \frac{3.15}{7} = 0.45 \\ \\ 5p + 4m = 8.25 \\ 5(0.45) + 4m = 8.25 \\ 2.25 + 4m = 8.25 \\ 4m = 8.25 - 2.25 \\ 4m = 6 \\ m = \frac{6}{4} \\ m = 1.5[/tex]

1 pear = $0,45

1 mango = $1,5
