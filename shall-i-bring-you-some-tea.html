5. accepting suggestions fan declining suggestions untuk pertanyaan nya shall i bring you some tea


accepting suggestion
x: shall i bring you some tea?
y: yes, please.

declining suggestion
x: shall i bring you some tea
y: no, thank you.. Accepting :
X : "Excuse me sir, you so tired. Shall I bring you some tea to refresh your mind?"
Y :"Yeah If you don't mind, but don't add too much sugar"
X : "Okay wait a minute"
Y "Thank you"

Decliining : 
A : "It's cold here. Shall I bring you some hot tea to warm up your body?"
B : "No thank you, I'm not in the good mood to drink"
A : "Okay then"

17. Tlong jawabkan Respon dari bahasa inggris1. Let's go the library2. I think you should go and meet her3. Can I help you ?4. Shall I bring you some tea ?5. Would you like another piece of cake ?​


Response:That’s good idea!Thank you for your suggestion Yes, you canYeah, why not Yes, I would

PEMBAHASAN

1. Let's go the library (Ayo pergi ke perpustakaan!)

=> That’s good idea (ide bagus)

Penjelasan:

Diajak ke perpustakaan bisa direspon dengan mengatakan bahwa itu adalah ide yang bagus

2. I think you should go and meet her (saya rasa kamu seharusnya pergi dan menemui dia)

=> Thank you for your suggestion (Terimakasih atas saranmu)

Penjelasan:

Saat diberi saran kita bisa mengucapkan terima kasih karena telah diberikan saran

3. Can I help you? (Bisa saya bantu?)

=> Yes, You can (iya boleh)

Penjelasan:

Ketika ditawari bantuan kita juga bisa menerima bantuannya

4. Shall I bring you some tea? (Mau saya ambilkan teh?)

=> Yeah, why not (iya, kenapa gak)

5. Would you like another piece of cake? (Kamu mau sepotong kue?)

=> Yes, I would (iya saya mau)

Penjelasan No. 4/5:

Ditawari makanan/minuman kita dengan senang hati menerimanya
