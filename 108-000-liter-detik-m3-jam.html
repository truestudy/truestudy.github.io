1. 108 000 liter/jam = .... m3/menit


Jawaban:

1,8 m³/menit

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

[tex]108.000 \: \frac{liter}{jam} \\ \\ = \frac{108.000 \: liter}{1 \: jam} \\ \\ = \frac{ \frac{108.000}{1.000} \: {m}^{3} }{60 \: menit} \\ \\ = \frac{108 \: {m}^{3} }{60 \: menit} \\ \\ = \frac{9}{5} \: \frac{ {m}^{3} }{menit} \\ \\ = \frac{9 \times 2}{5 \times 2} \: \frac{ {m}^{3} }{menit} \\ \\ = \frac{18}{10} \: \frac{ {m}^{3} }{menit} \\ \\ = 1.8 \: \frac{ {m}^{3} }{menit} [/tex]
