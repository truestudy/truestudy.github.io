14. nyatakan satuan dibawah ini dalam satuan besaran pokok



[tex]1000 \frac{g}{ {cm}^{3} } \times \frac{1kg}{1000g} \times \frac{1000000 {cm}^{3} }{1 {m}^{3} } = 1000000 \frac{kg}{ {m}^{3} } \\ 36 \frac{km}{jam} \times \frac{1000m}{1km} \times \frac{1jam}{3600s} = 10 \frac{m}{s} [/tex]
KONVERSI SATUAN

a.) 1000 gr/cm³ = ... kg/m³

ingat => 1 gr/cm³ = 1000 kg/m³

maka => 1000 gr/cm³
= 1000 . 1000
= 1.000.000 kg/m³
= 10⁶ kg/m³

b.) 36 km/jam = ... m/s
ingat => 1 km/jam = 10/36 m/s

maka => 36 km/jam
= 36 . 10/36
= 360/36
= 10 m/s
