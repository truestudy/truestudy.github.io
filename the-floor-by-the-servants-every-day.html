Dalam membentuk kalimat passive dengan tense simple present, rumusnya adalah"

subjek  + is/am/are + verb3  + by objek + keterangan dst

Jadi kalimat passive teks soal adalah:

subjek (The room)

+ is/am/are (kita pilih is) karena subjeknya the room (singular noun/kata benda tunggal)

+ verb 3 (cleaned => verb 3 dari clean)

+ by object (di sini objek nya adalah mommy, jadi by mommy)

+ keterangan ( di sini keterangannya: every day)

2) [+] I am writing an essay.

[-] I am not writing an essay.

[?] Are you writing an essay?

Penjelasan:

Kalimat nomor 1 menggunakan Simple Present Tense, dengan rumus umum:

[+] S + V.1 + ...

[-] S + don't / doesn't + V.1 + ...

[?] Do / does + S + V.1 + ...

Sedangkan, kalimat nomor 2 menggunakan Present Continuous Tense, dengan rumum umum:

[+] S + to be (is/am/are) + V.1 + -ing + ...

[-] S + to be (is/am/are) + not + V.1 + -ing + ...

[?] To be (is/am/are) + S + V.1 + -ing + ...

Pembahasan

Simple Present Tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan fakta umum (general fact), kebiasaan (habbitual), dan kegiatan sehari-hari (daily activity). Biasanya ditandai dengan:

Every ....OftenSeldomTodayAlwaysUssualyNever, etc

Rumus

➡️ Subject + verb 1 (s/es) + object.

Keterangan:

Jika subjek berupa she, he, it, singular noun maka perlu tambahan s/es.

Contoh:

I lock the door before going every morning.Fita always reads novel.You do homework every night.

30. the wrong sentence is.....A do the students always sweep the floor every Day? B do always the students sweep the floor every Day? C is the class always clean? D are you always in the class? ​


The answer is B. do always the students sweep the floor every Day?

EXPLANATION

Based on your question above, the wrong structure is the adverb of frequency sentence placement.

Dalam B. Indonesia, pertanyaan yg diajukan di atas, stuktur yang salah adalah penempatan keterangan frekuensi "always."

Here are the rules for the adverb of frequency placement :

1) If the sentence has only one verb, the adverb of frequency must be placed in the middle of the sentence. It means that we must put it after the subject and before the main verb.

Pattern :

S+ADVERB+Verb+......

Exp.

~ She always goes to Bali to visit her grandma.

2) The adverb of frequency usually comes after the auxiliary verb BE.

Pattern :

S+to be (is/am/are)+ADVERB......

Exp.

~ She isn't always correct

3) The adverb of frequency "always" must be put after the subject when we use it for the question form.

Pattern :

Helping verbs+ S+ADVERB+.......

Exp.

~ Does she always come late?

~ Is she always late ?

4) The adverb of frequency "always" can't be placed in the beginning or end of the sentence.

ELABORATION

Dari soal yg diajukan di atas, posisi keterangan frekuensi "always" yang benar adalah pada opsi A, C dan D.

Alasannya :

~ Sudah sesuai dengan struktur gramatikalnya dalam kalimat tanya. Penempatan "always" diletakkan setelah subyek (the students, the class and you)

Sedangkan,

Opsi B adalah salah karena tidak terstruktur dengan benar. Penempatan "Always" TIDAK BOLEH MENGAWALI subyeknya "the students."

Jadi, jawaban yang tepat adalah opsi B.
