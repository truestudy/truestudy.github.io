2. 720 hm/menit =…km/jam


720 hm/menit = … km/jam

Pembahasan :

Ukuran jarak :
km
.... hm
........ dam
.............. m
................. dm
...................... cm
........................... dm
catatan :
Tiap turun 1 tangga => dikali 10
Tiap naik 1 tangga => dibagi 10

Ukuran waktu
1 jam = 60 menit => 1 menit = 1/60 jam
1 menit = 60 detik => 1 detik = 1/60 menit
1 jam = 3.600 detik => 1 detik = 1/3.600 jam

Jadi

720 hm/menit
= (720 ÷ 10) km/menit
= 72 km/menit
[tex] = \frac{72 \: km}{menit} \\ \\ = \frac{72 \: km}{ \frac{1}{60} jam} \\ \\ = \frac{72}{ \frac{1}{60} } \: km/jam \\ \\ = 72 \: . \: \frac{60}{1} \: km/jam \\ \\ = 4320 \: km/jam[/tex]

a. 720 [tex]\frac{hm}{menit}[/tex] = 4.320 [tex]\frac{Km}{jam}[/tex]

b. 300 [tex]\frac{m}{menit}[/tex] = 500 [tex]\frac{cm}{detik}[/tex]

c. 4800 [tex]\frac{m}{menit}[/tex] = 288 [tex]\frac{Km}{jam}[/tex]

d. 3600 [tex]\frac{Km}{jam}[/tex] = 60.000 [tex]\frac{m}{menit}[/tex]

Pembahasan : Soal a :

720 [tex]\frac{hm}{menit}[/tex] = 720 × [tex]\frac{\frac{1}{10}~Km}{\frac{1}{60}~jam}[/tex]

= 720 × 6 [tex]\frac{Km}{jam}[/tex]

= 4.320 [tex]\frac{Km}{jam}[/tex]

Soal b :

300 [tex]\frac{m}{menit}[/tex] = 300 × [tex]\frac{100~cm}{60~detik}[/tex]

= 500 [tex]\frac{cm}{detik}[/tex]

Soal  c :

4800 [tex]\frac{m}{menit}[/tex] = 4800 × [tex]\frac{\frac{1}{1000}~Km}{\frac{1}{60}~jam}[/tex]

= 4800 × 0,06 [tex]\frac{Km}{jam}[/tex]

= 288 [tex]\frac{Km}{jam}[/tex]

Soal d :

3600 [tex]\frac{Km}{jam}[/tex] = 3600 × [tex]\frac{1000~m}{60~menit}[/tex]

= 60.000 [tex]\frac{m}{menit}[/tex]
